Economic Models of Identity Formation and Cultural Transmission

身份形成与文化传递的经济模型

Alberto Bisin, Thierry Verdier

NBER 工作论文 · 理论与方法 · 全球/多国

Abstract

In this paper we survey recent advances in the economic theory of identity formation. We restrict our analysis to models which conceptualize identity formation as embedded into an inter-generational cultural transmission process, and therefore have implications for the dynamics of social identity. Through the lens of a general framework linking identity formation to cultural evolution, we discuss issues related to the dynamics of oppositional, racial, or minority attitudes, cultural polarization, identity switching, and openness to alternative identities. An application of the set-up to multidimensional identities constructed from combinations of characteristics, provides conditions under which societies converge (or not) toward narrow identities centered on salient markers such as religion, ethnicity, or race. We conclude by outlining promising avenues for future research.

本文综述了身份形成经济理论的最新进展。我们将分析限定在将身份形成概念化为嵌入代际文化传递过程的模型，因此这些模型对社会认同的动态具有启示意义。通过一个将身份形成与文化演化联系起来的通用框架，我们讨论了与对立态度、种族或少数群体态度、文化极化、身份转换以及对替代身份开放性相关的动态问题。将该框架应用于由特征组合构建的多维身份，揭示了社会是（或不是）趋向于以宗教、族裔或种族等显著标志为中心的狭隘身份的条件。最后，我们概述了未来研究的有前景方向。

Global Financial Transparency and Offshore Wealth Accumulation

全球金融透明度与离岸财富积累

Annette Alstadsæter, Niels Johannesen, Ségal Le Guern Herry, Gabriel Zucman

NBER 工作论文 · 公共财政与税收 · 挪威

Abstract

This paper uses administrative data to analyze wealth flows from Norway to offshore tax havens before and after a major improvement of global financial transparency: automatic exchange of bank account information between tax authorities. We present three results suggesting that the policy is a significant deterrent of offshore tax evasion: First, taxpayers who become wealthy are much less likely to shift wealth to offshore banks. Second, when wealth does flow to offshore banks, it is much less likely to reduce domestic tax payments. Third, there is no increase in the use of complex ownership structures for offshore wealth flows.

中文摘要

Bank Runs With and Without Bank Failure

银行挤兑：银行倒闭与否

Sergio A. Correia, Stephan Luck, Emil Verner

NBER 工作论文 · 理论与方法 · 美国

Abstract

We study the causes and consequences of bank runs. By applying large language models to historical newspapers, we create a comprehensive database of bank runs in U.S. history with information on 3,984 runs on individual banks from 1863 to 1934. Our novel data allow us to establish that runs are considerably more likely in weak banks but also occur in strong banks, especially in response to negative news about the real economy or the broader banking system. However, runs typically only result in failure for banks with poor fundamentals. Strong banks survive runs through various mechanisms, including signaling strength, interbank cooperation, and temporary suspension. At the local level, runs on banks with poor fundamentals translate into substantially larger declines in deposits, lending, and manufacturing activity than runs on strong banks. Our findings imply that poor fundamentals are central to explaining both when runs occur and when they have severe economic effects, tempering the view that small shocks can generate discontinuous jumps to bad equilibria through self-fulfilling run dynamics.

我们研究银行挤兑的原因和后果。通过将大语言模型应用于历史报纸，我们创建了一个涵盖美国历史上银行挤兑的综合数据库，包含1863年至1934年间3984次针对单个银行的挤兑信息。我们的新数据使我们能够确定，挤兑在弱银行中发生的可能性要大得多，但也会发生在强银行中，尤其是在有关实体经济或更广泛银行体系的负面消息出现时。然而，挤兑通常只会导致基本面较差的银行倒闭。强银行通过各种机制（包括信号传递实力、银行间合作和临时暂停）在挤兑中幸存。在地方层面，对基本面较差银行的挤兑导致的存款、贷款和制造业活动下降幅度远大于对强银行的挤兑。我们的研究结果表明，基本面较差是解释挤兑何时发生以及何时产生严重经济影响的核心因素，这削弱了关于小冲击可通过自我实现的挤兑动态导致向坏均衡的非连续跳跃的观点。

Who Wants to Break Up Big Firms? Harm, Fairness, and the Demand for Antitrust

谁想拆分大企业？损害、公平与反垄断需求

Ricardo Perez-Truglia, Jeffrey Yusof

NBER 工作论文 · 其他主题 · 美国

Abstract

The rise of superstar firms has made dominant companies central to modern economic life, and antitrust enforcement is one of the main policy tools for regulating their market power. Public opinion can shape the political and regulatory environment in which antitrust enforcement takes place, yet there is little direct evidence on what drives these preferences. We conduct a pre-registered information-provision experiment with 4,000 American households. Respondents were told about one of five real antitrust cases and randomly assigned to information treatments designed to study four potential drivers of support for antitrust enforcement: perceived market share, perceived consumer harm, perceived unfair competition, and perceived negative image. All four treatments moved the beliefs they were designed to affect, but their effects on demand for antitrust differed sharply. Information about consumer harm had the most systematic effects: it increased plaintiff support and support for break-up and conduct remedies, with effects remaining visible one month later, and also spilled over to broader support for antitrust policies. By contrast, and contrary to expert forecasts, information about market share had no meaningful effect on demand for antitrust enforcement. The findings suggest that the public thinks like economists in one key respect: they do not care about market share per se, but respond instead to consumer harm. One factor outside the core economic framework, perceived unfair competition, also matters, though its effects are more limited in scope. We discuss implications for policymakers and regulators.

中文摘要

超级明星企业的崛起使主导公司成为现代经济生活的核心，反垄断执法是监管其市场势力的主要政策工具之一。公众舆论可以塑造反垄断执法所处的政治和监管环境，然而，关于是什么驱动了这些偏好的直接证据却很少。我们对4000个美国家庭进行了一项预先注册的信息提供实验。受访者被告知五个真实反垄断案例之一，并被随机分配到旨在研究支持反垄断执法的四个潜在驱动因素的信息处理组：感知市场份额、感知消费者损害、感知不公平竞争和感知负面形象。所有四种处理都改变了它们旨在影响的信念，但它们对反垄断需求的影响差异显著。关于消费者损害的信息具有最系统的影响：它增加了对原告的支持以及对拆分和行为补救措施的支持，这些影响在一个月后仍然可见，并且还溢出到对更广泛反垄断政策的支持。相比之下，与专家预测相反，关于市场份额的信息对反垄断执法需求没有有意义的影响。研究结果表明，公众在一个关键方面像经济学家一样思考：他们不关心市场份额本身，而是对消费者损害做出反应。核心经济框架之外的一个因素——感知不公平竞争——也很重要，尽管其影响范围更为有限。我们讨论了对政策制定者和监管者的启示。

Physician Learning and Forgetting: Evidence from the US Military

医生的学习与遗忘：来自美国军方的证据

Trevor Fitzgerald, Atul Gupta, Stephen D. Schwab

NBER 工作论文 · 环境与健康 · 美国

Abstract

Physicians in the U.S. military health care system are quasi-randomly assigned to combat deployments, leading to gaps of varying durations in their clinical practice. We leverage this unique setting to provide novel causal evidence on skill depreciation (forgetting) and learning-by-doing among physicians. We find robust evidence that readmission rates increase by 48% in the first two months after a surgeon returns from a deployment. However, surgeons return to baseline performance within 6 months of resuming practice. In contrast, obstetricians maintain elevated cesarean section rates for up to 9 months, implying a deeper change in clinical judgment. Finally, we interpret these patterns through the lens of a learning model and estimate learning-by-doing rates for surgeons and obstetricians. A doubling of recent procedure volume reduces readmission and C-section rates by 17% and 11% for surgeons and obstetricians, respectively.

中文摘要

美国军事医疗系统中的医生被准随机分配到战斗部署任务，导致其临床实践出现不同时长的间断。我们利用这一独特环境，提供了关于医生技能折旧（遗忘）和干中学的新因果证据。我们发现强有力的证据表明，外科医生从部署返回后的头两个月内，再入院率增加了48%。然而，外科医生在恢复实践后的6个月内恢复到基线表现。相比之下，产科医生维持升高的剖宫产率长达9个月，这表明临床判断发生了更深层次的变化。最后，我们通过一个学习模型的视角解释这些模式，并估计了外科医生和产科医生的干中学率。近期手术量翻倍分别使外科医生和产科医生的再入院率和剖宫产率降低17%和11%。

Human Capital in Venture Capital: Evidence From 100,000 Venture Capitalists

风险投资中的人力资本：来自10万名风险投资家的证据

Blake Jackson, Ilya A. Strebulaev

NBER 工作论文 · 金融与资产定价 · 美国

Abstract

We study human capital in venture capital (VC) using a new dataset covering over 100,000 professionals affiliated with U.S. VC firms. Investment success is extremely concentrated: fewer than 40% of VCs with any investments are ever credited with a successful investment, and 90% of investment profits are generated by 5% of VCs. Differences in education, prior work experience, and demographics predict career progression and investment outcomes, consistent with persistent investor-specific skills. Quasi-experimental variation from marginal inclusions on the Forbes Midas List shows that achieving superstar status increases access to highly-valued startups, complementing other human capital and contributing to concentration.

中文摘要

我们利用一个涵盖超过10万名隶属于美国风险投资公司专业人士的新数据集，研究风险投资中的人力资本。投资成功高度集中：在任何投资的风险投资家中，不到40%曾被记录有成功投资，90%的投资利润由5%的风险投资家创造。教育背景、先前工作经验和人口统计特征的差异预测了职业发展和投资结果，这与持续的投资者特定技能一致。来自福布斯Midas榜单边缘入选者的准实验变化表明，获得超级明星地位增加了接触高估值初创企业的机会，这补充了其他人力资本并促进了集中度。

Volatility Disagreement in the Options Market

期权市场中的波动率分歧

Turan G. Bali, Bryan T. Kelly, Mathis Mörke

NBER 工作论文 · 理论与方法 · 未指明区域

Abstract

We construct a novel stock-level measure of volatility disagreement as the cross-sectional dispersion of realized variance forecasts built from heterogeneous information sets and machine learning models. Sorting single-name delta-hedged straddles on this measure yields a long-short return of −5.14% per month, robust to known option return predictors and not subsumed by disagreement about first moments or the variance risk premium. A one-standard-deviation increase in volatility disagreement is associated with a 30% rise in option position opening. The cross-sectional patterns of our disagreement measure align with recent theoretical advances of beliefs and pricing of variance claims. Evidence on attention, ownership, and arbitrage costs is more consistent with mispricing than risk compensation.

中文摘要

我们构建了一种新颖的股票层面波动率分歧度量，即基于异质信息集和机器学习模型所构建的已实现方差预测的横截面离散度。根据该度量对单名股票德尔塔对冲跨式期权进行排序，可产生每月-5.14%的多空收益，该结果对已知的期权收益预测因子具有稳健性，且不能被关于一阶矩或方差风险溢价的分歧所解释。波动率分歧增加一个标准差，与期权头寸开仓量上升30%相关。我们分歧度量的横截面模式与近期关于方差索取权信念和定价的理论进展相一致。关于注意力、所有权和套利成本的证据更支持错误定价而非风险补偿。

Spreading Out Across Expanding Idea Space

在扩张的思想空间中扩散分布

Ina Ganguli, Jeffrey Lin, Vitaly Meursault, Nicholas F. Reynolds

NBER 工作论文 · 宏观与货币财政 · 美国

Abstract

Over nearly two centuries, US inventions have become increasingly dissimilar: not just fewer head-to-head collisions between inventors, but growing distance between neighboring inventions. We document this secular decline in similarity using validated neural language models applied to the full text of claims in over 11 million US patents (1836–2023), corroborated by a 98% decline in patent interference rates, a measure of independent simultaneous invention. Measuring this correctly requires validation, since different representations of the same patent text can yield opposite conclusions about whether inventions are converging or spreading out. Our validation framework, the first systematic comparison for patent text, selects among these representations. We develop a spatial competition model in which inventors choose locations in idea space. The model explains spreading out and connects it to several independently documented patterns — rising R&D investment per inventor, increasing patent values, weakening knowledge spillovers, and declining research productivity. The mechanism is spatial; as inventors spread out to capture new territory, inventions become more valuable but also more costly for others to absorb. In doing so, the model turns spillover intensity, innovation step size, and research productivity from fixed primitives into outcomes of inventor positioning. A calibrated decomposition attributes roughly 40% of the long-run decline in US research productivity to these spatial forces, alongside traditional explanations such as fishing out and the burden of knowledge. Where inventors stand relative to each other in idea space matters as much for growth as how many of them there are.

中文摘要

在近两个世纪的时间里，美国发明之间的相似度持续下降：不仅发明者之间的正面碰撞减少，而且相邻发明之间的距离也在扩大。我们利用经过验证的神经语言模型，对超过1100万件美国专利（1836–2023年）的权利要求全文进行分析，记录了这种相似度的长期下降趋势，并通过专利冲突率（一种独立同时发明的度量指标）下降98%加以佐证。正确测量这一现象需要验证，因为同一专利文本的不同表征可能得出关于发明是趋同还是扩散的相反结论。我们的验证框架是首个针对专利文本的系统性比较，用于在这些表征中进行选择。我们开发了一个空间竞争模型，其中发明者在思想空间中选择位置。该模型解释了扩散现象，并将其与若干独立记录的模式联系起来——每位发明者研发投资上升、专利价值增加、知识溢出减弱以及研究生产率下降。其机制是空间性的；随着发明者扩散以占领新领域，发明变得更有价值，但他人吸收的成本也更高。在此过程中，该模型将溢出强度、创新步长和研究生产率从固定的原始参数转变为发明者定位的结果。一项校准分解表明，美国研究生产率的长期下降中约有40%可归因于这些空间力量，同时还有传统解释如“捕捞殆尽”和知识负担。在思想空间中，发明者之间的相对位置对增长的重要性不亚于发明者的数量。

Optimal Currency Strategies Under Deviations From Interest Parity

利率平价偏离下的最优货币策略

Luis M. Viceira, Sally Shen

NBER 工作论文 · 金融与资产定价 · 全球/多国

Abstract

This paper examines optimal currency demands for global equity and bond investors in a large cross-section of developed and emerging markets over the 1975-2023 period. It extends the framework of Campbell et al. (2010) by incorporating both optimal portfolio-risk minimizing currency exposures, accounting for empirically measured hedging costs arising from deviations of Covered Interest Parity (CIP), and optimal expected-return-driven currency demands based on non-zero expected excess currency returns arising from empirically measured deviations of Uncovered Interest Parity (UIP). The analysis shows that the main conclusions of their portfolio risk-minimizing framework hold for this larger and longer panel of countries and that they are robust to deviations from CIP. Specifically, it is optimal for portfolio risk-minimizing equity investors to hold exposures to the U.S. dollar and the euro while avoiding exposure to all other currencies, whereas bond investors should hedge all currency exposures. In contrast, observed deviations from UIP are sufficiently large and persistent among currencies with high average relative interest currencies, especially Emerging Markets currencies, to generate expected return-driven currency demands that offset, and in some cases reverse, portfolio-risk minimizing demands, even for investors with low risk tolerance. The average excess returns on those currencies are large enough to compensate investors for their substantial return volatility and strong positive covariance with equity returns.

中文摘要

本文研究了1975–2023年期间全球股票和债券投资者在发达市场和新兴市场大横截面中的最优货币需求。它扩展了Campbell等人（2010）的框架，纳入了最优投资组合风险最小化的货币敞口（考虑了由抵补利率平价（CIP）偏离产生的经验测量对冲成本），以及基于非零预期超额货币收益（由非抵补利率平价（UIP）偏离的经验测量产生）的最优预期收益驱动型货币需求。分析表明，其投资组合风险最小化框架的主要结论对于这个更大、时间更长的国家面板仍然成立，并且对CIP偏离具有稳健性。具体而言，对于投资组合风险最小化的股票投资者而言，最优策略是持有美元和欧元的敞口，同时避免所有其他货币的敞口；而债券投资者则应对冲所有货币敞口。相比之下，在具有高平均相对利率的货币（尤其是新兴市场货币）中，观察到的UIP偏离足够大且持续，足以产生预期收益驱动型货币需求，这些需求抵消甚至在某些情况下逆转了投资组合风险最小化的需求，即使对于风险容忍度较低的投资者也是如此。这些货币的平均超额收益足够大，足以补偿投资者其显著的收益波动性以及与股票收益的强正协方差。

Exorbitant Privilege of the Periodic Table? Geoeconomics, Endogenous Centrality and Strategic Minerals for the Green Transition

元素周期表的过度特权？地缘经济学、内生中心性与绿色转型的战略矿产

Josh Kirk, Evgenia Passari, Hélène Rey

NBER 工作论文 · 国际贸易 · 美国

Abstract

Abstract
