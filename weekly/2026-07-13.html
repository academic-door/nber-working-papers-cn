Supply and Demand with Market Heterogeneity

市场异质性下的供给与需求

Ingvil Gaarder, Lancelot Henry de Frahan, Magne Mogstad, Alexander Torgovitsky, Oscar Volpe

来源：NBER 官方网站 API · 公共财政与税收 · 美国

Abstract

We revisit the classic identification problem of separating supply and demand for a homogeneous good using data from multiple markets. We allow markets to be heterogeneous according to unobservables, a feature that arises if there are unobservable differences in consumer preferences or firm technology. We develop a new identification analysis based on hypothetical market types. We use this analysis to show how nonparametric, economically motivated assumptions carry empirical restrictions for a wide range of target parameters, including elasticities, but also welfare parameters, such as consumer surplus. Then, we develop computationally tractable methods for implementing partially identified linear random coefficients models in which the slopes of supply and demand are heterogeneous. We apply these methods to estimate the welfare impact and incidence of sales taxes in the United States.

中文摘要

我们重新审视了利用多个市场数据分离同质商品供给与需求的经典识别问题。我们允许市场根据不可观测因素存在异质性，这一特征源于消费者偏好或企业技术中不可观测的差异。我们基于假设的市场类型发展了一种新的识别分析方法。利用这一分析，我们展示了非参数、经济动机驱动的假设如何对广泛的目标参数施加经验约束，这些参数不仅包括弹性，还包括福利参数（如消费者剩余）。随后，我们开发了计算上可行的方法，用于实现部分识别的线性随机系数模型，其中供给和需求的斜率是异质的。我们将这些方法应用于估算美国销售税的福利影响和归宿。

Women in the Platform Economy: Descriptive Evidence from Drivers in India and Indonesia

平台经济中的女性：来自印度和印尼司机的描述性证据

Achyuta Adhvaryu, Levina Adiputri, Valentina Brailovskaya, Priyanka Dua, Jenny Susan John, Pratibha Joshi, Terry Muthahhari, Rivandra Royono, Jack Thunde

来源：NBER 官方网站 API · 教育与劳动 · 印度

Abstract

We provide descriptive evidence on women's experiences in platform-based driving and delivery work using survey and administrative data from India and Indonesia. Partnering with major two-wheeler platforms, we study representative samples of drivers, oversampling women to examine gender differences (India: 404 women, 2,153 men; Indonesia: 892 women, 2,114 men). Female participation is extremely low -- 0.8% in India and 1.5% in Indonesia -- consistent with barriers related to safety concerns, cultural norms, and limited access to required assets. Among women who do enter, platform work is used differently than by men: women work fewer hours, are more likely to log in during free time, and place greater value on flexibility and supplemental earnings. Women earn less per hour than men -- by 6-8% in India and 8-22% in Indonesia -- differences that align with gendered choices over working times, locations, and service types, likely shaped by safety and domestic constraints. Women also face distinct challenges: higher reported harassment in both countries and, in Indonesia, widespread customer discrimination and higher accident rates. At the same time, a disproportionate share of women were unemployed before joining the platform, and women are more likely than men to report higher current earnings relative to prior work. Together, the evidence highlights how flexibility coexists with persistent gender-specific risks, constraints, and unequal returns in location-based platform work in low- and middle-income country contexts.

中文摘要

我们利用印度和印尼的调查与行政数据，提供了关于女性在平台化驾驶和配送工作中经历的描述性证据。通过与主要两轮车平台合作，我们研究了具有代表性的司机样本，并超额抽样女性以考察性别差异（印度：404名女性，2153名男性；印尼：892名女性，2114名男性）。女性参与率极低——印度为0.8%，印尼为1.5%——这与安全担忧、文化规范以及获取所需资产受限等障碍一致。在已进入平台的女性中，她们对平台工作的使用方式与男性不同：女性工作时间更少，更倾向于在空闲时间登录，并且更看重灵活性和补充性收入。女性每小时收入低于男性——印度低6-8%，印尼低8-22%——这些差异与性别化的工作时间、地点和服务类型选择相一致，这些选择很可能受到安全和家庭约束的影响。女性还面临独特的挑战：两国均报告了更高的骚扰率，在印尼还存在普遍的客户歧视和更高的事故率。与此同时，加入平台前失业的女性比例过高，且女性比男性更有可能报告当前收入高于先前工作。综合来看，这些证据凸显了在中低收入国家的基于位置平台工作中，灵活性如何与持续的性别特定风险、约束以及不平等的回报并存。

What Do Asset Prices in April 2025 Say About Demand for the Dollar?

2025年4月的资产价格揭示了美元需求的什么信息？

Rohan Kekre, Moritz Lenel

来源：NBER 官方网站 API · 国际贸易 · 美国

Abstract

We interpret exchange rate and yield curve responses to the April 2, 2025 U.S. tariff announcement through the lens of an equilibrium model capturing the portfolio balance mechanism. Disciplined by price elasticities from QE announcements, a 3-12% decline in the demand for dollar bonds (relative to annual U.S. GDP) accounts for the dollar depreciation and rise in dollar yields during this episode. News of a gradual rebalancing out of dollar bonds is consistent with immediate price impact since asset prices are forward-looking. Lower dollar bond demand was accompanied by higher risk aversion, which explains the cross-section of responses across currencies.

中文摘要

我们通过一个捕捉投资组合平衡机制的均衡模型，解读汇率和收益率曲线对2025年4月2日美国关税公告的反应。在量化宽松公告的价格弹性约束下，美元债券需求下降3-12%（相对于美国年度GDP）解释了这一时期美元贬值和美元收益率上升。关于逐步减持美元债券的消息与即时的价格影响一致，因为资产价格具有前瞻性。美元债券需求下降伴随着风险厌恶情绪上升，这解释了不同货币之间反应的横截面差异。

Social Insurance with Imperfect Eligibility Screening: Theory and Evidence from Pandemic UI

具有不完全资格筛查的社会保险：来自疫情失业保险的理论与证据

Adam Isen, Elira Kuka, Bryan A. Stuart

来源：NBER 官方网站 API · 金融与资产定价 · 未指明区域

Abstract

This paper studies social insurance with imperfect eligibility screening, focusing on Unemployment Insurance (UI) during its expansion in 2020 and 2021. We study the extent of imperfect screening by identifying anomalous payments using administrative tax data and UI policies, finding $214 billion in potentially-improper payments—concentrated in the Pandemic Unemployment Assistance (PUA) program—with approximately half detectable ex-ante through improved federal-state data sharing. There is substantial geographic variation, and a border design shows this is partly due to policy decisions made by states. To assess the implications for optimal policy, we first conduct simulations that replace PUA with means-tested, lump-sum transfers, finding that these transfers would have better insured against income losses at lower administrative cost. Second, we develop a model of opt-in versus automatic transfers that shows the targeting advantage of opt-in programs can reverse when ineligible recipients pass the benefit screen. Calibrated to 2020 UI, the model implies that shifting toward automatic transfers would have increased social welfare.

中文摘要

本文研究了具有不完全资格筛查的社会保险，重点关注2020年和2021年扩张期间的失业保险。我们通过利用行政税务数据和失业保险政策识别异常支付，来考察不完全筛查的程度，发现2140亿美元的潜在不当支付——主要集中在疫情失业援助计划中——其中约一半可通过改进联邦-州数据共享在事前检测到。存在显著的地理差异，边界设计表明这在一定程度上源于各州的政策决策。为评估对最优政策的影响，我们首先进行模拟，用基于经济状况调查的定额转移支付替代疫情失业援助计划，发现这些转移支付能以更低的行政成本更好地对冲收入损失。其次，我们构建了一个关于选择加入与自动转移支付的模型，表明当不合格领取者通过福利筛查时，选择加入计划的目标定位优势可能逆转。以2020年失业保险为校准，该模型表明转向自动转移支付将增加社会福利。

Long Term Care and Cognitive Impairment in Spain

西班牙的长期护理与认知障碍

Joan Costa-i-Font, Sergi Jimenez-Martin, Juan Oliva, Cristina Vilaplana-Prieto, Analía Viola

来源：NBER 官方网站 API · 环境与健康 · 未指明区域

Abstract

The growing prevalence of cognitive impairment (CI) is one of the main drivers of age-related demand for health and long-term care (LTC). In Spain, CI is estimated to affect 18.5% of Spaniards over 65, and 45.3% in those aged 85 and above. This paper draws on a pooled pre-COVID data from a longitudinal sample of individuals aged 65+ to examine the effect of CI and physical limitations on health and long-term care utilisation, estimates its costs, and financial burden. We report four sets of findings. First, we find that socioeconomic status at older age to be the strongest predictor of CI. Second, while both CI and physical limitations increase health and care adult care use, physical impairment is a stronger predictor of overall care utilisation (73% versus 55% for CI alone) and nursing home residence (2.0% versus 0.9%). Third, informal caregiving constitutes the overwhelming majority of dementia costs, accounting for 69–81% of the total. Finally, we estimate that the replacement cost of informal care would exhaust the full budget of Spain’s LTC system (SAAD).

中文摘要

认知障碍患病率的上升是年龄相关健康与长期护理需求的主要驱动因素之一。在西班牙，估计18.5%的65岁以上西班牙人受认知障碍影响，85岁及以上人群中这一比例达45.3%。本文利用疫情前合并的纵向数据样本（65岁以上个体），考察认知障碍和身体限制对健康与长期护理利用的影响，估算其成本及财务负担。我们报告四组发现。第一，老年时期的社会经济地位是认知障碍的最强预测因子。第二，虽然认知障碍和身体限制均增加健康与成人护理使用，但身体障碍是整体护理利用（仅认知障碍为55%，身体障碍为73%）和养老院居住（2.0%对0.9%）的更强预测因子。第三，非正式照护构成痴呆症成本的绝大多数，占总成本的69-81%。最后，我们估算非正式照护的替代成本将耗尽西班牙长期护理系统的全部预算。

Mind the KF* Gap: EME Vulnerabilities to Global Shocks

警惕KF*缺口：新兴市场经济体对全球冲击的脆弱性

John D. Burger, Francis E. Warnock, Veronica Cacdac Warnock

来源：NBER 官方网站 API · 其他主题 · 全球/多国

Abstract

Emerging market economies (EMEs) have historically been vulnerable to external shocks. Recently the global economy has experienced several major shocks yet EMEs have been remarkably resilient. Some of this improved performance can be attributed to prudent policies and stronger economic fundamentals, but the existing literature points to an overperformance mystery by some EMEs with weaker fundamentals. We evaluate an alternative measure of vulnerability based on the concept of a natural level of capital flows (KF*). Specifically, we hypothesize that EMEs are more vulnerable when prior to a large global shock capital flows—portfolio inflows, to be specific—exceed KF*. Results indicate that for major global shocks over the past two decades the pre-shock gap between actual portfolio and KF* has substantial predictive power for the post-shock performance of EMEs.

中文摘要

新兴市场经济体历史上易受外部冲击影响。近期全球经济经历多次重大冲击，但新兴市场经济体表现出显著的韧性。这种改善的部分表现可归因于审慎的政策和更强的经济基本面，但现有文献指出，一些基本面较弱的新兴市场经济体存在表现超预期的谜题。我们基于资本流动自然水平的概念，评估了一种替代性的脆弱性衡量指标。具体而言，我们假设当重大全球冲击发生前，资本流动（特别是证券投资流入）超过KF*时，新兴市场经济体更为脆弱。结果表明，对于过去二十年间的重大全球冲击，冲击前实际证券投资与KF*之间的缺口对新兴市场经济体冲击后的表现具有显著的预测能力。

Psychosocial Health and Well-Being After A Mortality Shock

死亡率冲击后的社会心理健康与福祉

Elizabeth Frankenberg, Cecep Sumantri, Duncan Thomas

来源：NBER 官方网站 API · 环境与健康 · 印度

Abstract

We investigate the short- and long-run impacts of unexpected mortality on psychosocial health in the context of a large-scale high-mortality natural disaster, the 2004 Indian Ocean earthquake and tsunami. The effects of the tsunami-related mortality measured at the community level are contrasted with the effects of individual-specific loss of close kin using population-representative longitudinal survey data from the Study of the Tsunami Aftermath and Recovery. In the short-run, two years post-tsunami, both community and individual-level mortality exposures are significant predictors of elevated depression and post-traumatic stress symtoms. In the longer-run, ten years post-tsunami, individual-level exposures are slightly attenuated but remain significant predictors of both psychosocial health measures; in contrast, community-level mortality predicts post-traumatic stress but not depression symptoms. Mortality is linked to smaller household sizes in the short- and long-run and to less social support in the long-run, although the latter effect differs substantially for males and females. The estimates adjust for other tsunami exposure measures and, in the individual-level analyses, we compare people living in the same community at the time of the tsunami who were exposed to the same community-level mortality rate.

中文摘要

我们以2004年印度洋地震和海啸这一大规模高死亡率自然灾害为背景，研究了意外死亡率对社会心理健康的短期和长期影响。利用“海啸后果与恢复研究”中具有人口代表性的纵向调查数据，我们将社区层面测量的海啸相关死亡率的影响与个体层面近亲丧失的影响进行了对比。在短期（海啸后两年），社区和个体层面的死亡率暴露都是抑郁和创伤后应激症状升高的显著预测因子。在长期（海啸后十年），个体层面的暴露效应略有减弱，但仍是对两种社会心理健康指标的显著预测因子；相比之下，社区层面的死亡率能预测创伤后应激症状，但不能预测抑郁症状。死亡率与短期和长期的家庭规模缩小有关，并与长期的社会支持减少有关，尽管后一种效应在男性和女性之间存在显著差异。我们的估计对其他海啸暴露指标进行了调整，并且在个体层面分析中，我们比较了海啸发生时居住在同一社区、面临相同社区死亡率的人群。

Should We Tax Trade? A Pigouvian Perspective

我们应该对贸易征税吗？一个庇古视角

Arnaud Costinot, Iván Werning

来源：NBER 官方网站 API · 公共财政与税收 · 全球/多国

Abstract

We develop a simple and intuitive Pigouvian perspective on optimal trade policy. Our approach unifies a wide range of rationales for taxing trade, from the classical optimal tariff argument to contemporary debates about global carbon emissions and geopolitics. We also clarify when trade policy intervention is warranted and when alternative domestic instruments should be used instead.

中文摘要

Multi-Project Collaborations

多项目合作

Charles Angelucci, Roi Orzach

来源：NBER 官方网站 API · 其他主题 · 未指明区域

Abstract

We analyze collaborative experimentation across multiple independent domains. Each domain contains infinitely many potential projects with asymmetric benefits. In each period and in each domain, two players can idle, jointly explore a new project, or jointly exploit a known one, with voluntary transfers. For intermediate discount factors, treating domains as independent during experimentation is suboptimal. The optimal experimentation policy exhibits common features of collaborative experimentation: lengthy exploration, temporary project exploitation, recall of past projects, and inefficient initial or terminal idling within certain domains. We connect these findings to research on buyer-supplier dynamics and persistent productivity differences.

中文摘要

我们分析了跨多个独立领域的合作性实验。每个领域包含无限多个具有不对称收益的潜在项目。在每个时期和每个领域，两个参与者可以选择闲置、共同探索一个新项目，或共同利用一个已知项目，并允许自愿转移支付。对于中等贴现因子，在实验过程中将各领域视为独立是次优的。最优实验政策展现出合作性实验的共同特征：漫长的探索、暂时的项目利用、对过去项目的召回，以及在某些领域内低效的初始或最终闲置。我们将这些发现与关于买方-供应商动态和持续生产率差异的研究联系起来。

From Stocks to Flows: Debt Service and Fiscal Sustainability

从存量到流量：债务服务与财政可持续性

Barry Eichengreen, Maxime Menuet, Gregory Donnat

来源：NBER 官方网站 API · 宏观与货币财政 · 美国

Abstract

We revisit fiscal sustainability through the lens of the government’s budget constraint. What constrains fiscal policy is not the stock of outstanding liabilities per se, but the fiscal cost of servicing debt. Using two centuries of U.S. fiscal data (1800–2023) and a long-run panel of advanced economies, we show that primary surpluses are systematically more closely associated with debt-service burdens, while debt ratios lose explanatory power when debt service is taken into account. Fiscal responses intensify when financing conditions deteriorate, specifically when the interest-growth differential is positive. We rationalize these findings using a simple flow-based framework in which debt stabilization depends on the responsiveness of fiscal surpluses to financing pressures. The results suggest that fiscal sustainability depends less on debt thresholds than on financing regimes and governments’ ability to absorb debt-service burdens.

中文摘要

我们通过政府预算约束的视角重新审视财政可持续性。约束财政政策的并非未偿债务存量本身，而是偿还债务的财政成本。利用美国两个世纪的财政数据（1800-2023年）和一个发达经济体的长期面板数据，我们表明，基本盈余与债务服务负担的系统性关联更为紧密，而当考虑债务服务时，债务比率会失去解释力。当融资条件恶化时，特别是当利率-增长率差为正时，财政反应会加剧。我们用一个简单的基于流量的框架来合理化这些发现，在该框架中，债务稳定取决于财政盈余对融资压力的反应程度。结果表明，财政可持续性更多地取决于融资体制和政府吸收债务服务负担的能力，而非债务阈值。

Abstract

Firms often rebrand to counter negative shocks, but can it work? Using a historical natural experiment, we analyze a large sample of companies from the same industry (banking) that shared very similar names, but overwhelmingly decided to change them in response to a common, negative news shock. U.S. entry into World War I in 1917 created a sudden anti-German backlash against anything that invoked an association with the now enemy. Abstract

Discrete choice experiments are widely used to estimate workers’ willingness to pay (WTP) for job amenities under the assumption that varying an attribute does not change workers’ beliefs about other job attributes. We test this assumption by embedding an amenity with a known market price—a popular monthly public transport pass—in a largescale discrete choice experiment with German workers. Many workers, including public transport users, overvalue the ticket by more than 100%, despite WTP for other attributes aligning with the literature. Abstract

Both historically and today, support of aging parents has largely taken the form of in-kind transfers that require physical proximity, such as housing and caregiving. If Social Security substitutes for such support, it can relax constraints on where recipients' children live and work. We investigate the long-run intergenerational effects of the early Social Security program, exploiting within-occupation, cross-industry differences in coverage and a new dataset linking parents to their children's later-life outcomes. We propose that the early program enabled families to realize gains from migration that were back-loaded, uncertain, and difficult to contract on.

Abstract

We study how residential-commercial zoning affects the allocation of urban space. Abstract

Abstract

In the canonical view of over-the-counter markets, dealers intermediate single-asset trades one at a time. We study a complementary role: when investors trade several assets at once, dealers absorb the joint position into inventory, insuring against execution risk. Using data on the near-universe of Canadian fixed-income trades, we find that bundled transactions account for 20 percent of investor volume. We develop a simple model of why, when, where, and at what prices investors bundle, and use it to explain three patterns. Bundling rises with volatility, when insurance against execution risk is most valuable, and the two types sort asymmetrically across bilateral and electronic venues. Together, these patterns reveal a dimension of intermediation the single-asset view overlooks, and one likely to grow in importance.
