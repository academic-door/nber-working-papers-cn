Misreporting in the Norwegian Business Cash Support Scheme

挪威企业现金支持计划中的虚报行为

Dinara Alpysbayeva, Annette Alstadsæter, Wojciech Kopczuk, Simen Markussen, Oddbjorn Raaum

公共财政与税收 未指明区域

Abstract

We analyze the reporting response to an ambitiously targeted government support scheme for Norwegian businesses at the very start of the Coronavirus crisis\nin 2020. Our empirical design is based on cross-checking self-reported data in the applications for support with administratively reported data used for VAT. We\nfind strong evidence that strategic misreporting was present but conclude that its remaining quantitative extent after enforcement actions already taken by the tax authorities was relatively small. Firms tend to misreport 4 percent more often than expected, and the actual support paid out was 5 percent higher than it should have been. We discuss possible reasons for the relatively limited extent of non-compliance and more general lessons for the design of transfer programs

中文摘要

我们分析了在2020年新冠疫情危机初期，企业对挪威一项目标精准的政府支持计划的报告反应。我们的实证设计基于将支持申请中的自我报告数据与用于增值税的行政报告数据进行交叉核对。我们发现了战略性虚报存在的有力证据，但得出结论，在税务机关已采取的执法行动之后，其剩余的量化程度相对较小。企业虚报的频率比预期高出4%，实际支付的支持金额比应有水平高出5%。我们讨论了不遵从行为相对有限的可能原因，以及这对转移支付计划设计的更普遍启示。
