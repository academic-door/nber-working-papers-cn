NBER Working Paper w32700所在周报所在月度合集

Moral Hazard and Unemployment in Competitive Equilibrium

竞争均衡中的道德风险与失业

Patrick Rey, Joseph E. Stiglitz

公共财政与税收 未指明区域

Abstract

Principal-agent models take outside options, determining participation and incentive constraints, as given. We construct a general equilibrium model where workers’ reservation wages and the maximum punishment acceptable before workers quit are instead determined endogenously. We simultaneously extend the standard effort efficiency-wage model by incorporating noisy signals, labor market frictions, and the possibility of performance-based pay, analyzing the equilibrium response to an adverse signal, and establishing conditions under which equilibrium entails lowering wages (performance contracting) rather than firing. We provide a complete analysis of the general equilibrium comparative statics, showing, for instance, that frictions (sand-in-the-wheels) may decrease unemployment and that the equilibrium is determined by two simple aggregates which depend on the parameters of the economy, interpretable as the intercept and slope of a pseudo-labor supply curve, embedding all the binding constraints (e.g., the no-shirking and labor market participation constraints). We also show that there may exist only a firing equilibrium, a no-firing equilibrium, multiple (firing and no-firing) equilibria, and no pure-strategy equilibrium. The economy is, in general, not efficient either in the selection of the form of equilibrium or the wages paid within any type of equilibrium. We discuss welfare enhancing government interventions, including publicly provided sand-in-the-wheels.

中文摘要

委托代理模型将决定参与约束和激励约束的外部选择视为给定。我们构建了一个一般均衡模型，其中工人的保留工资和工人辞职前可接受的最大惩罚是内生的。我们同时通过引入噪声信号、劳动力市场摩擦以及基于绩效的薪酬可能性，扩展了标准的努力效率工资模型，分析了均衡对不利信号的反应，并建立了均衡是降低工资（绩效合同）而非解雇的条件。我们提供了对一般均衡比较静态的完整分析，例如，表明摩擦（齿轮中的沙子）可能降低失业率，并且均衡由两个简单的总量决定，这两个总量取决于经济参数，可解释为伪劳动力供给曲线的截距和斜率，其中嵌入了所有约束条件（例如，不偷懒约束和劳动力市场参与约束）。我们还表明，可能只存在解雇均衡、不解雇均衡、多重（解雇和不解雇）均衡，以及不存在纯策略均衡。经济在均衡形式的选择或任何类型均衡内支付的工资方面，通常都不是有效率的。我们讨论了增进福利的政府干预，包括公共提供的“齿轮中的沙子”。
