The Role of Private Debt in the Financial Ecosystem

私人债务在金融生态系统中的作用

Victoria Ivashina

宏观与货币财政 未指明区域

Abstract

This paper provides a comprehensive introduction to private debt, examines the key drivers behind its expansion, and frames the discussion around potential risk accumulation in the broader economy. Overall, the development of private debt can be viewed as the emergence of a new technology that facilitated the expansion of the high-yield corporate debt market into the middle market. Throughout its history, the evolution of the high-yield debt and the rise of private debt have been closely intertwined with the growth of private equity. Historically, banks have not had significant exposure to financing buyouts or other highly leveraged transactions, whether in the large-cap or mid-cap segments.

中文摘要

本文对私人债务进行了全面介绍，考察了其扩张背后的关键驱动因素，并围绕更广泛经济中潜在风险积累的框架展开讨论。总体而言，私人债务的发展可被视为一种新技术的出现，该技术促进了高收益公司债务市场向中端市场的扩张。纵观其历史，高收益债务的演变和私人债务的兴起一直与私募股权的增长紧密交织。从历史上看，无论是在大盘股还是中盘股领域，银行对收购融资或其他高杠杆交易的敞口并不显著。
