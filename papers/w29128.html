The Treasury Market in Spring 2020 and the Response of the Federal Reserve

2020年春季的国债市场与美联储的应对

Annette Vissing-Jorgensen

金融与资产定价 未指明区域

Abstract

Treasury yields spiked during the initial phase of COVID. The 10-year yield increased by 64 bps from March 9 to 18, 2020, leading the Federal Reserve to purchase $\$1$T of Treasuries in 2020Q1. Fed purchases were causal for reducing Treasury yields based on the timing of purchases (which increased on March 19), the timing of yield reversal and Fed purchases in the MBS market, and evidence against confounding factors. Treasury-QE worked more via purchases than announcements. The yield spike was driven by liquidity needs of mutual funds, foreign official agencies, and hedge funds that were unaffected by the March 15 Treasury-QE announcement.

中文摘要

在新冠疫情初期，国债收益率飙升。10年期收益率在2020年3月9日至18日期间上升了64个基点，促使美联储在2020年第一季度购买了1万亿美元的国债。基于购买时机（3月19日购买量增加）、收益率反转的时机以及美联储在抵押贷款支持证券市场的购买行为，并排除了混杂因素的证据，美联储的购买行为是降低国债收益率的原因。量化宽松政策通过购买行为而非公告发挥了更大作用。收益率飙升是由共同基金、外国官方机构和对冲基金的流动性需求驱动的，这些需求并未受到3月15日国债量化宽松公告的影响。
