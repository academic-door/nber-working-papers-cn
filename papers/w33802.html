Convergence? Thoughts About the Evolution of Mainstream Macroeconomics Over the Last 40 Years

趋同？关于过去40年主流宏观经济学演变的思考

Olivier J. Blanchard

其他主题 未指明区域

Abstract

This year marks the 40th anniversary of the NBER Macro Annual Conference, founded in 1986. This paper reviews the evolution of mainstream macroeconomics since then. It presents my views, informed by a survey of a number of researchers who have made important contributions to the field. I develop two main arguments.\nThe first is that, starting from strikingly different positions, there has been substantial convergence, in terms of methodology, architecture, and main mechanisms. Methodology: Explicit micro foundations, explicit treatment of distortions, with, at the same time, an increased willingness to deviate from rational expectations, neoclassical utility and profit maximization. Architecture: The wide acceptance of nominal rigidities as an essential distortion, although with mixed feelings. Mechanisms: The wide nature of the shocks to both the demand and the supply side.\nThe second is that this convergence has been, for the most part, good convergence, i.e. the creation of a generally accepted conceptual and analytical structure, a core to which additional distortions can be added, allowing for discussions and integration of new ideas and evidence, rather than fights about basic methodology. Not everything is right however, with too much emphasis on general equilibrium implications from the start, rather than, first, on partial equilibrium analysis of the phenomenon at hand. \n\nThe appendix to the paper gives a sample of the views of the members of the survey on each of these arguments.

中文摘要

今年是NBER宏观年度会议创办40周年（始于1986年）。本文回顾了自那时起主流宏观经济学的演变。本文阐述了我的观点，这些观点基于对多位为该领域做出重要贡献的研究人员的调查。我提出了两个主要论点。第一个论点是，从截然不同的立场出发，在方法论、架构和主要机制方面已出现实质性趋同。方法论：明确的微观基础，对扭曲的明确处理，同时更愿意偏离理性预期、新古典效用和利润最大化。架构：广泛接受名义刚性作为一种基本扭曲，尽管态度复杂。机制：需求侧和供给侧冲击的广泛性质。第二个论点是，这种趋同在很大程度上是良好的趋同，即建立了一个普遍接受的概念和分析结构，一个可以添加额外扭曲的核心，允许讨论和整合新思想与证据，而非围绕基本方法论的争论。然而，并非一切皆正确，从一开始就过度强调一般均衡含义，而非首先对当前现象进行局部均衡分析。论文附录给出了调查成员对每个论点看法的样本。
