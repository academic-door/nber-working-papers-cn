Can Mentoring Help Female Assistant Professors in Economics? An Evaluation by Randomized Trial

导师制能否帮助经济学领域的女性助理教授？一项随机试验的评估

Donna K. Ginther, Janet Currie, Francine D. Blau, Rachel Croson

其他主题 未指明区域

Abstract

Women continue to be underrepresented in academic ranks in the economics profession. The Committee on the Status of Women in the Economics Profession of the American Economics Association established the CeMENT mentoring workshop to support women in research careers. The program was designed as a randomized controlled trial. This study evaluates differences between the treatment and control groups in career outcomes. Results indicate that relative to women in the control group, treated women are more likely to stay in academia and more likely to have received tenure in an institution ranked in the top 30 or 50 in economics in the world.\n

中文摘要

在经济学界，女性在学术职位中的代表性仍然不足。美国经济学会经济学界女性地位委员会设立了CeMENT导师制研讨会，以支持女性从事研究职业。该项目被设计为一项随机对照试验。本研究评估了处理组和对照组在职业成果上的差异。结果表明，与对照组中的女性相比，接受处理的女性更有可能留在学术界，并且更有可能在全球经济学排名前30或前50的机构中获得终身教职。
