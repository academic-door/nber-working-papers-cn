Combining Matching and Synthetic Control to Trade oﬀ Biases from Extrapolation and Interpolation

结合匹配与合成控制以权衡外推偏差与插值偏差

Maxwell Kellogg, Magne Mogstad, Guillaume Pouliot, Alexander Torgovitsky

国际贸易 未指明区域

Abstract

The synthetic control method is widely used in comparative case studies to adjust for diﬀerences in pre-treatment characteristics. A major attraction of the method is that it limits extrapolation bias that can occur when untreated units with diﬀerent pre-treatment characteristics are combined using a traditional adjustment, such as a linear regression. Instead, the SC estimator is susceptible to interpolation bias because it uses a convex weighted average of the untreated units to create a synthetic untreated unit with pre-treatment characteristics similar to those of the treated unit. More traditional matching estimators exhibit the opposite behavior: they limit interpolation bias at the potential expense of extrapolation bias. We propose combining the matching and synthetic control estimators through model averaging to create an estimator called MASC. We show how to use a rolling-origin cross-validation procedure to train the MASC to resolve trade-oﬀs between interpolation and extrapolation bias. We use a series of empirically-based placebo and Monte Carlo simulations to shed light on when the SC, matching, MASC and penalized SC estimators do (and do not) perform well. Then, we use the MASC re-examine the economic costs of conﬂicts and ﬁnd evidence of larger eﬀects than with SC.

中文摘要

合成控制法在比较案例研究中被广泛用于调整处理前特征的差异。该方法的一个主要吸引力在于，它限制了当使用传统调整方法（如线性回归）组合具有不同处理前特征的未处理单元时可能出现的外推偏差。相反，合成控制估计量容易受到插值偏差的影响，因为它使用未处理单元的凸加权平均值来创建一个处理前特征与处理单元相似的合成未处理单元。更传统的匹配估计量表现出相反的行为：它们以潜在的外推偏差为代价来限制插值偏差。我们提出通过模型平均将匹配估计量与合成控制估计量相结合，创建一种称为MASC的估计量。我们展示了如何使用滚动起点交叉验证程序来训练MASC，以解决插值偏差和外推偏差之间的权衡。我们使用一系列基于经验的安慰剂和蒙特卡洛模拟来阐明合成控制、匹配、MASC和惩罚合成控制估计量何时表现良好（以及何时不表现良好）。然后，我们使用MASC重新审视冲突的经济成本，并发现比使用合成控制法时更大的效应证据。
