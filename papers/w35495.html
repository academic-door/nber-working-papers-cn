Human Enhancement Technologies: A Survey Experiment on Private Demand and Governance Preferences.

人类增强技术：关于私人需求与治理偏好的调查实验

Giovanni Immordino, Mario Macis, Immacolata Marino, Fabrizio Panebianco

公共财政与税收 美国

Abstract

When a new technology promises large private benefits but may impose social costs that markets do not price, demand need not reveal how citizens want it governed. We examine this using a nationally representative U.S. survey experiment (N=5,556) on human enhancement technologies (HET). The experiment randomizes benefit domain, mechanism, heritability, purpose, and risk across vignettes; for each respondent’s assigned vignette, we elicit stated adoption, preferred regulation, and ethical and societal concerns. Overall, about 53% would adopt. Framing the technology as enhancing rather than restorative lowers adoption by about five percentage points, as much as a severe side-effect profile. About 28% would not adopt at any benefit. This refusal is driven overwhelmingly by the enhancing framing rather than by risk, consistent with a non-compensatory constraint for a substantial subgroup. Most who would adopt still favor strict regulation, and most who would never adopt do not wish to forbid others from doing so. Productivity enhancement generates the most ethical concern of any attribute but attracts the least regulation, and respondents favor subsidizing rather than taxing its adoption, consistent with a concern about access rather than safety. Private demand is therefore an unreliable guide to the governance citizens want, and the divergence we document provides a basis for regulators seeking to align the direction of technical change with societal values and priorities.

中文摘要

当一项新技术承诺巨大的私人收益，但可能带来市场无法定价的社会成本时，需求并不一定能揭示公民希望如何对其进行治理。我们通过一项具有全国代表性的美国调查实验（N=5,556）来研究这一问题，该实验聚焦于人类增强技术（HET）。实验在情境描述中随机化收益领域、机制、可遗传性、目的和风险；针对每位受访者分配的情境，我们获取其声称的采纳意愿、偏好的监管方式以及伦理和社会关切。总体而言，约53%的人会采纳。将技术描述为增强型而非恢复型会使采纳率下降约5个百分点，其影响与严重的副作用特征相当。约28%的人在任何收益水平下都不会采纳。这种拒绝主要是由增强型框架而非风险驱动的，这与一个相当大子群体的非补偿性约束一致。大多数愿意采纳的人仍然支持严格监管，而大多数永远不会采纳的人并不希望禁止他人这样做。在所有属性中，生产力增强引发的伦理关切最多，但吸引的监管最少，且受访者倾向于补贴而非征税以促进其采纳，这与对可及性而非安全性的关切一致。因此，私人需求并非公民期望治理方式的可靠指南，我们记录的这种分歧为寻求使技术变革方向与社会价值观和优先事项相一致的监管者提供了基础。
