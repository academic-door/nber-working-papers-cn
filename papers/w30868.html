A Survey of Private Debt Funds

私募债务基金调查

Joern Block, Young Soo Jang, Steven N. Kaplan, Anna Schulze

金融与资产定价 美国

Abstract

Despite its large and increasing size in the U.S. and Europe, there is relatively little research on the private debt (PD) market, particularly compared to the bank and syndicated loan markets. Accordingly, in this paper, we survey U.S. and European investors with private debt assets under management (AuM) of over $\$300$ billion. These investors are primarily direct lending funds. We ask the general partners (GPs) how they source, select, and evaluate deals, how they think of private debt relative to bank and syndicated loan financing, how they monitor their investments, how they interact with private equity (PE) sponsors and how they view the future of the market. The respondents provide primarily cash flow-based loans and believe that they finance companies and leverage levels that banks would not fund. The direct lending funds target unlevered returns that appear high relative to their risk. They use leverage in their funds, but appreciably less than banks and collateralized loan obligation funds (CLOs). They use and negotiate for both financial and incurrence covenants to monitor their investments. The presence of PE sponsors helps them lend more and craft more effective covenants. U.S. and European funds are similar on many dimensions, but the European funds rely less on PE sponsors and compete more with banks. Overall, the private debt market is both different from, but shares characteristics with the bank loan and syndicated loan markets.

中文摘要

尽管私募债务（PD）市场在美国和欧洲规模庞大且不断增长，但相关研究相对较少，尤其是与银行贷款市场和银团贷款市场相比。因此，在本文中，我们调查了美国和欧洲管理私募债务资产超过3000亿美元的投资者。这些投资者主要是直接贷款基金。我们向普通合伙人（GP）询问他们如何寻找、筛选和评估交易，如何看待私募债务相对于银行贷款和银团贷款融资，如何监控投资，如何与私募股权（PE）发起人互动，以及如何看待市场未来。受访者主要提供基于现金流的贷款，并认为他们为银行不会提供融资的公司和杠杆水平提供资金。直接贷款基金的目标无杠杆回报率相对于其风险而言似乎较高。他们在基金中使用杠杆，但明显低于银行和担保贷款凭证基金（CLOs）。他们使用并协商财务契约和发生契约来监控投资。PE发起人的存在帮助他们发放更多贷款并制定更有效的契约。美国和欧洲的基金在许多方面相似，但欧洲基金对PE发起人的依赖较少，与银行的竞争更多。总体而言，私募债务市场既不同于银行贷款和银团贷款市场，又与之共享一些特征。
