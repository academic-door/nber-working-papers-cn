The Value of Statistical Life: A Meta-analysis of Meta-analyses

统计生命价值：一项元分析的元分析

H. Spencer Banzhaf

环境与健康 未指明区域

Abstract

The Value of Statistical Life (VSL) is arguably the most important number in benefit-cost analyses of environmental, health, and transportation policies. However, agencies have used a wide range of VSL values. One reason may be the embarrassment of riches when it comes to VSL studies. While meta-analysis is a standard way to synthesize information across studies, we now have multiple competing meta-analyses and reviews. Thus, to analysts, picking one such meta-analysis may feel as hard as picking a single "best study." This paper responds by taking the meta-analysis another step, estimating a meta-analysis (or mixture distribution) of six meta-analyses. The baseline model yields a central VSL of $\$7.0$m, with a 90% confidence interval of $\$2.4$m to $\$11.2$m. The provided code allows users to easily change subjective weights on the studies, add new studies, or change adjustments for income, inflation, and latency.

中文摘要

统计生命价值（VSL）可以说是环境、健康和交通政策成本效益分析中最重要的数字。然而，各机构使用的VSL值范围很广。一个原因可能是VSL研究数量过多。虽然元分析是综合各项研究信息的标准方法，但我们现在有多个相互竞争的元分析和综述。因此，对于分析人员来说，选择其中一个元分析可能和选择单个“最佳研究”一样困难。本文通过将元分析再推进一步，对六项元分析进行元分析（或混合分布）来回应这一问题。基准模型得出的中心VSL为700万美元，90%置信区间为240万至1120万美元。提供的代码允许用户轻松更改对各项研究的主观权重、添加新研究，或调整收入、通货膨胀和潜伏期的影响。
