Zoomers and Boomers: Asset Prices and Intergenerational Inequality

Zoomers与Boomers：资产价格与代际不平等

Leland Farmer, Roger Farmer

金融与资产定价 美国

Abstract

We construct a perpetual youth DSGE model with aggregate uncertainty in which there are dynamically complete markets and agents have Epstein-Zin preferences. We prove that, when endowments have a realistic hump-shaped age-profile, our model has three steady-state equilibria. One of these equilibria is dynamically inefficient and displays real price indeterminacy. We estimate the parameters of our model and we find that a fourth-order approximation around the indeterminate steady-state provides the best fit to U.S. data. Our work interprets the large and persistent generational inequality that has been observed in western economies over the past century as the result of uninsurable income shocks to birth cohorts.

中文摘要

我们构建了一个具有总体不确定性的永续青年DSGE模型，其中存在动态完备市场，且代理人具有Epstein-Zin偏好。我们证明，当禀赋具有现实的驼峰形年龄分布时，我们的模型存在三个稳态均衡。其中一个均衡是动态无效率的，并表现出实际价格的不确定性。我们估计了模型的参数，并发现围绕不确定稳态的四阶近似对美国数据提供了最佳拟合。我们的工作将过去一个世纪西方经济体中观察到的大规模且持久的代际不平等解释为出生队列面临的不可保险收入冲击的结果。
