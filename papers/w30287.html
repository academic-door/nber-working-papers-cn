Help Really Wanted? The Impact of Age Stereotypes in Job Ads on Applications from Older Workers

真的需要帮助吗？招聘广告中的年龄刻板印象对老年工作者申请的影响

Ian Burn, Daniel Firoozi, Daniel Ladd, David Neumark

环境与健康 未指明区域

Abstract

Correspondence studies have found evidence of age discrimination in callback rates for older workers, but less is known about whether job advertisements can themselves shape the age composition of the applicant pool. We construct job ads for administrative assistant, retail, and security guard jobs, using language from real job ads collected in a prior large-scale correspondence study (Neumark et al., 2019a). We modify the job-ad language to randomly vary whether or not the job ad includes ageist language regarding age-related stereotypes. Our main analysis relies on machine learning methods to design job ads based on the semantic similarity between phrases in job ads and age-related stereotypes. In contrast to a correspondence study in which job searchers are artificial and researchers study the responses of real employers, in our research the job ads are artificial and we study the responses of real job searchers. \nWe find that job-ad language related to ageist stereotypes, even when the language is not blatantly or specifically age-related, deters older workers from applying for jobs. The change in the age distribution of applicants is large, with significant declines in the average and median age, the 75th percentile of the age distribution, and the share of applicants over 40. Based on these estimates and those from the correspondence study, and the fact that we use real-world ageist job-ad language, we conclude that job-ad language that deters older workers from applying for jobs can have roughly as large an impact on hiring of older workers as direct age discrimination in hiring.

中文摘要

通信研究已发现老年工作者在回电率方面存在年龄歧视的证据，但关于招聘广告本身是否能塑造申请者的年龄构成，我们知之甚少。我们为行政助理、零售和保安职位构建了招聘广告，使用了先前一项大规模通信研究（Neumark et al., 2019a）中收集的真实招聘广告的语言。我们修改了招聘广告的语言，随机变化广告是否包含与年龄相关刻板印象有关的年龄歧视性语言。我们的主要分析依赖于机器学习方法，根据招聘广告中的短语与年龄相关刻板印象之间的语义相似性来设计招聘广告。与通信研究中求职者是虚构的、研究人员研究真实雇主的反应不同，在我们的研究中，招聘广告是虚构的，我们研究真实求职者的反应。我们发现，与年龄歧视性刻板印象相关的招聘广告语言，即使这些语言并非明显或专门与年龄相关，也会阻止老年工作者申请工作。申请者年龄分布的变化很大，平均年龄和中位年龄、年龄分布的第75百分位数以及40岁以上申请者的比例均显著下降。基于这些估计以及通信研究的估计，并且考虑到我们使用了现实世界中的年龄歧视性招聘广告语言，我们得出结论：阻止老年工作者申请工作的招聘广告语言对老年工作者招聘的影响，大致与招聘中的直接年龄歧视一样大。
