Utility and Happiness

效用与幸福感

Miles S. Kimball, Robert J. Willis

环境与健康 未指明区域

Abstract

Psychologists have developed effective survey methods of measuring how happy people feel at a given time. The relationship between how happy a person feels and utility is an unresolved question. Existing work in Economics either ignores happiness data or assumes that felt happiness is more or less the same thing as flow utility. The approach we propose in this paper steers a middle course between the two polar views that “happiness is irrelevant to Economics” and the view that “happiness is a sufficient statistic for utility.” \nWe argue that felt happiness is not the same thing as flow utility, but that it does have a systematic relationship to utility. In particular, we propose that happiness is the sum of two components: (1) elation–or short-run happiness–which depends on recent news about lifetime utility and (2) baseline mood–or long-run happiness–which is a subutility function much like health, entertainment, or nutrition. In principle, all of the usual techniques of price theory apply to baseline mood, but the application of those techniques is complicated by the fact that many people may not know the true household production function for baseline mood. \nIf this theory is on target, there are two reasons data on felt happiness is important for Economics. First, short-run happiness in response to news can give important information about preferences. Second, long-run happiness is important for economic welfare in the same way as other higher-order goods such as health, entertainment, or nutrition.

中文摘要

心理学家已开发出有效的调查方法来测量人们在特定时刻感受到的幸福程度。一个人感受到的幸福与效用之间的关系是一个尚未解决的问题。经济学现有的研究要么忽视幸福感数据，要么假设感受到的幸福大致等同于流量效用。本文提出的方法在“幸福感与经济学无关”和“幸福感是效用的充分统计量”这两种极端观点之间走了一条中间路线。我们认为，感受到的幸福并不等同于流量效用，但它确实与效用存在系统性关联。具体而言，我们提出幸福感由两个部分组成：(1) 兴奋感——或短期幸福感——它取决于关于终身效用的近期信息；(2) 基线情绪——或长期幸福感——它是一种类似于健康、娱乐或营养的子效用函数。原则上，所有价格理论的常规技术都适用于基线情绪，但这些技术的应用因许多人可能不知道基线情绪的真实家庭生产函数这一事实而变得复杂。如果这一理论是准确的，那么感受到的幸福数据对经济学之所以重要有两个原因。首先，对信息做出反应的短期幸福感可以提供关于偏好的重要信息。其次，长期幸福感对于经济福利的重要性，与健康、娱乐或营养等其他高阶商品相同。
