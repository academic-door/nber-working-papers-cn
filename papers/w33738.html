The Importance of Preventive Medical Care for Managing Chronic Disease

预防性医疗保健对管理慢性病的重要性

Claire Boone, Pablo A. Celhay, Paul Gertler, Tadeja Gracner

环境与健康 未指明区域

Abstract

We study how preventive medical care use affects health behaviors and outcomes for patients with chronic diseases. Leveraging variation induced by a national appointment reminder program, rolled out across 315 public primary care clinics in Chile, we use an instrumental variables approach with patient-level administrative data from over 300,000 patients with type 2 diabetes or hypertension. We find that increased preventive visits lead to more screening tests and large increases in medication adherence. Preventive care also leads to earlier detection and treatment of cardiovascular complications; we document an increase in cardiovascular hospitalizations but a reduction in in-hospital mortality.

中文摘要

我们研究预防性医疗保健的使用如何影响慢性病患者的健康行为与结果。利用智利315家公立初级保健诊所推行的全国性预约提醒计划所引发的变异，我们采用工具变量方法，分析了超过30万名2型糖尿病或高血压患者的个体层面行政数据。我们发现，增加预防性就诊次数会导致更多的筛查检测以及药物依从性的大幅提升。预防性医疗保健还导致心血管并发症的早期发现和治疗；我们记录了心血管住院率的增加，但住院死亡率有所下降。
