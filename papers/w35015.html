Market Power in Mortgage Pricing: the Role of Referral Lending

抵押贷款定价中的市场势力：推荐贷款的作用

Dayin Zhang, Panle Jia Barwick, Lu Han, Jonathan Kroah

其他主题 未指明区域

Abstract

Despite intense competition among mortgage lenders, borrowers face substantial price dispersion. We argue that realtor–loan officer referral networks are a key source of lender market power: by steering homebuyers toward a small set of loan officers, these networks restrict effective borrower choice even in competitive markets. Using a novel dataset linking 81,306 realtors to 102,860 loan officers across 41 states, we document that such networks are pervasive and highly concentrated — 85% of realtors direct over 40% of their clients to fewer than four loan officers — and that this concentration persists and even increases in markets with more lenders. IV estimates indicate that borrowers using referred loan officers pay 18.6 basis points higher mortgage rates, equivalent to $\$2,609$ in upfront costs on the average loan of $\$306$K. Referral lending raises rate spreads by 36.5% and accounts for half of the residual cross-sectional variation in spreads. The premium is nearly three times as large for Hispanic borrowers as for White borrowers, and is systematically higher for Black borrowers and financially constrained households. We identify two channels: referrals reduce borrowers' search intensity across lenders, and referred loan officers exercise pricing power relative to colleagues within the same institution. Efficiency gains from faster processing and reduced denial risk do not offset the additional costs.

中文摘要

尽管抵押贷款机构之间存在激烈竞争，但借款人面临显著的价格离散。我们认为，房地产经纪人-贷款官员推荐网络是贷款机构市场势力的一个关键来源：通过将购房者引导至一小部分贷款官员，这些网络即使在竞争激烈的市场中也限制了借款人的有效选择。利用一个将41个州的81,306名房地产经纪人与102,860名贷款官员联系起来的新数据集，我们记录了此类网络普遍存在且高度集中——85%的房地产经纪人将超过40%的客户引导至少于四名贷款官员——并且这种集中度在拥有更多贷款机构的市场中持续存在甚至增加。IV估计表明，使用推荐贷款官员的借款人支付的抵押贷款利率高出18.6个基点，相当于平均30.6万美元贷款中2,609美元的前期成本。推荐贷款使利率利差提高了36.5%，并解释了利差横截面残差变异的一半。对于西班牙裔借款人，这一溢价几乎是白人借款人的三倍，并且对于黑人借款人和财务受限家庭系统性更高。我们识别出两个渠道：推荐减少了借款人在不同贷款机构间的搜索强度，并且被推荐的贷款官员相对于同一机构内的同事行使定价权。更快处理和降低拒绝风险带来的效率提升并不能抵消额外成本。
