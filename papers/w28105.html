Beyond 140 Characters: Introduction to The Role of Innovation and Entrepreneurship in Economic Growth

超越140个字符：创新与创业在经济增长中的作用导论

Michael J. Andrews, Aaron Chatterji, Scott Stern

宏观与货币财政 未指明区域

Abstract

This is an introduction to the forthcoming volume "The Role of Innovation and Entrepreneurship in Economic Growth." The chapters collected in this volume seek to answer the following questions: What is the relationship between innovation/entrepreneurship and economic growth in specific industrial sectors? How has the relationship between innovation /entrepreneurship and economic growth changed over time? How much do policies, programs, and specialized institutions meant to encourage innovation or entrepreneurship ultimately spur economic growth? Does innovation or entrepreneurship affect economic performance and social progress other than through measured productivity and economic growth, and if so, how can these effects be measured? We synthesize the chapters in this volume and present broad conclusions.

中文摘要

这是对即将出版的《创新与创业在经济增长中的作用》一书的导论。本书收集的章节旨在回答以下问题：在特定产业部门中，创新/创业与经济增长之间的关系是什么？创新/创业与经济增长之间的关系如何随时间变化？旨在鼓励创新或创业的政策、项目和专门机构在多大程度上最终刺激了经济增长？除了通过衡量的生产率和经济增长之外，创新或创业是否还影响经济表现和社会进步？如果是，这些影响如何衡量？我们综合了本书各章节的内容，并提出了广泛的结论。
