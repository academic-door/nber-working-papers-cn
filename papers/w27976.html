The (Missing) Relation Between Acquisition Announcement Returns and Value Creation

收购公告收益与价值创造之间的（缺失）关系

Itzhak Ben-David, Utpal Bhattacharya, Stacey E. Jacobsen

理论与方法 未指明区域

Abstract

Cumulative abnormal returns (CAR) computed around acquisition announcements are widely considered to be market-based assessments of expected value creation. We show, however, that announcement returns do not correlate with commonly used and new measures of ex-post outcomes. A simple characteristics-based model using standard information known at the announcement date can predict these outcomes reasonably well, yet CAR even fails to capture the predictions from this model. Evidence suggests that information about the standalone acquirer dominates CAR, making it virtually impossible to extract deal-related information. We conclude that CAR is an unreliable measure of expected value creation.

中文摘要

围绕收购公告计算的累积异常收益（CAR）被广泛视为基于市场的预期价值创造评估。然而，我们表明，公告收益与常用及新的事后结果衡量指标并不相关。一个使用公告日已知标准信息的简单基于特征的模型可以相当好地预测这些结果，但CAR甚至未能捕捉到该模型的预测。证据表明，关于独立收购方的信息主导了CAR，使得提取交易相关信息几乎不可能。我们得出结论，CAR是预期价值创造的一个不可靠的衡量指标。
