What Are the Benefits of Metro Rail in Mumbai, India?

印度孟买地铁的效益是什么？

Palak Suri, Maureen L. Cropper

其他主题 印度

Abstract

We estimate the effects of the first metro rail line in Mumbai using administrative data on assessed property prices from 2011-18 for 723 subzones in the city. Comparing areas within 1 km of the metro with those beyond 1 km but within 3 km, we estimate the effects on property values for commercial, industrial, and residential properties. We find a significant and persistent increase in prices of 6-8% for residential and commercial land use categories in treated areas relative to control areas after Metro Line 1 opens. We show that commute time savings and improvements in employment accessibility are plausible mechanisms underlying these effects.

中文摘要

我们利用2011-2018年孟买市723个分区评估物业价格的行政数据，估算了该市首条地铁线路的影响。通过比较距地铁1公里以内的区域与距地铁1至3公里以内的区域，我们估算了地铁对商业、工业和住宅物业价值的影响。我们发现，在1号地铁线开通后，相对于控制区域，处理区域内的住宅和商业用地类别的价格出现了显著且持续的6-8%上涨。我们表明，通勤时间节省和就业可达性的改善是这些影响背后的合理机制。
