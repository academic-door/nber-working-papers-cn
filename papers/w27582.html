Using Tax Data to Better Capture Top Incomes in Official UK Income Inequality Statistics

利用税收数据更好地捕捉英国官方收入不平等统计中的顶层收入

Dominic Webber, Richard P. Tonkin, Martin Shine

公共财政与税收 英国

Abstract

It is widely recognised that household surveys do not fully capture the incomes of the very richest individuals and households, particularly those among the so-called “top 1%”, for reasons including non-response and under-reporting. As a consequence, estimates based on survey data alone typically understate true levels of inequality.

中文摘要

人们普遍认识到，家庭调查未能完全捕捉最富裕个人和家庭的收入，尤其是那些所谓的“前1%”人群，原因包括无回应和低报。因此，仅基于调查数据的估计通常会低估真实的收入不平等水平。
