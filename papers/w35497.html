Exorbitant Privilege of the Periodic Table? Geoeconomics, Endogenous Centrality and Strategic Minerals for the Green Transition

元素周期表的过度特权？地缘经济学、内生中心性与绿色转型的战略矿产

Josh Kirk, Evgenia Passari, Hélène Rey

国际贸易 中国相关 美国

Abstract

Trade networks underpinning the energy transition are endogenous economic objects, and their structure is a source of market power. Using bilateral trade data for thirteen electrification metals over 1995–2023, we show that trade in these materials has reorganised into a hub-and-spoke system centred on China, in sharp contrast to the diffuse, multilateral structure of fossil fuels. This centrality did not follow from resource endowments: China holds few of the underlying reserves. It was built through processing investment, industrial policy, and commodity-targeted development finance—an equilibrium outcome rather than a geographical accident. We formalise this in a stylised model in which a country chooses how central to become, and show that centrality maps into pricing power: the world price response to a strategic supply cut is stronger when the network is more concentrated and downstream demand and fringe supply are more inelastic. Constructing text-based, commodity-specific supply shocks and estimating local projections, we find that supply contractions in critical metals raise US and EU consumer prices by roughly twice as much as comparable fossil-fuel shocks, and more persistently. Centrality is therefore not merely descriptive: it creates leverage over global prices. The green transition reduces strategic dependence on hydrocarbon exporters but reconstitutes it around mineral supply chains.

中文摘要

支撑能源转型的贸易网络是内生的经济对象，其结构是市场势力的来源。利用1995–2023年间十三种电气化金属的双边贸易数据，我们表明这些材料的贸易已重组为一个以中国为中心的轴辐式体系，这与化石燃料分散的多边结构形成鲜明对比。这种中心性并非源于资源禀赋：中国持有的相关储量很少。它是通过加工投资、产业政策和针对特定商品的发展融资建立起来的——这是一种均衡结果，而非地理上的偶然。我们在一个简约模型中对此进行了形式化，在该模型中，一个国家选择其中心化程度，并表明中心性映射为定价权：当网络更加集中，且下游需求和边缘供给更加缺乏弹性时，世界价格对战略性供给削减的反应更强。通过构建基于文本的、特定商品供给冲击并估计局部投影，我们发现关键金属的供给收缩使美国和欧盟的消费者价格上升幅度大约是可比化石燃料冲击的两倍，且更具持续性。因此，中心性不仅仅是描述性的：它创造了影响全球价格的杠杆。绿色转型减少了对碳氢化合物出口国的战略依赖，但将其重新构建在矿产供应链周围。
