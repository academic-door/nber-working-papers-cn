Current Account Dynamics and the Saving-Investment Nexus In a Changing and Uncertain World

变化与不确定世界中的经常账户动态与储蓄-投资关系

Menzie D. Chinn, Hiro Ito

环境与健康 美国

Abstract

We re‐examine the determinants of current account balances (CAB) and the saving-investment nexus with focus on emerging market and developing economies (EMDEs). We are in a new age in terms of facing not just economic challenges but also other non-economic challenges such as global climate changes, increasing natural disasters, and wars. We face the need to reexamine the determinants of CAB along with national saving and investment. We first take an event study approach, examining how these variables have evolved historically in the wake of wars, natural disasters, and pandemics. The second is a cross‐country panel investigation of CAB, national saving, and of investment. In the presence of global financial instability, EMDEs tend to experience an improvement in CAB due to a fall in investment. A rise in oil prices increases both national saving and investment, but the change in investment is greater than the change in national saving, which worsens CAB. Contractionary monetary policy by the U.S. Federal Reserve Board tends to lower both national saving and investment, but the impact on CAB is not statistically different from zero. The more frequently a country experiences wars, on average, its CAB tends to improve. When a climatological or geographical disaster occurs, its CAB, national saving, and investment tend to improve. A rise in the level of U.S. monetary policy uncertainty leads to an improvement in CAB, mainly due to a fall in investment.

中文摘要

我们重新审视了经常账户余额的决定因素以及储蓄-投资关系，重点关注新兴市场和发展中经济体。我们正处在一个新时代，不仅面临经济挑战，还面临其他非经济挑战，如全球气候变化、日益增多的自然灾害和战争。我们需要重新审视经常账户余额以及国民储蓄和投资的决定因素。我们首先采用事件研究方法，考察这些变量在战争、自然灾害和大流行之后的历史演变。其次是关于经常账户余额、国民储蓄和投资的跨国面板研究。在全球金融不稳定的情况下，新兴市场和发展中经济体由于投资下降，经常账户余额往往改善。油价上涨同时增加了国民储蓄和投资，但投资的变化大于国民储蓄的变化，从而恶化了经常账户余额。美联储的紧缩性货币政策倾向于降低国民储蓄和投资，但对经常账户余额的影响在统计上不显著。一个国家经历战争的频率越高，其经常账户余额平均而言往往改善。当发生气候或地理灾害时，其经常账户余额、国民储蓄和投资往往改善。美国货币政策不确定性水平的上升导致经常账户余额改善，这主要是由于投资下降。
