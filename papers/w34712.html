Artificial Jagged Intelligence: When AI Benchmarks Misstate Deployment Value

人工锯齿智能模型

Joshua S. Gans

AI 与数字经济 未指明区域

Abstract

Generative AI systems often display highly uneven performance across tasks that appear “nearby”: they can be excellent on one prompt and confidently wrong on another with only small changes in wording or context. We call this phenomenon Artificial Jagged Intelligence (AJI). This paper develops a tractable economic model of AJI that treats adoption as an information problem: users care about local reliability, but typically observe only coarse, global quality signals. In a baseline one-dimensional landscape, truth is a rough Brownian process, and the model “knows” scattered points drawn from a Poisson process. The model interpolates optimally, and the local error is measured by posterior variance. We derive an adoption threshold for a blind user, show that experienced errors are amplified by the inspection paradox, and interpret scaling laws as denser coverage that improves average quality without eliminating jaggedness. We then study mastery and calibration: a calibrated user who can condition on local uncertainty enjoys positive expected value even in domains that fail the blind adoption test. Modelling mastery as learning a reliability map via Gaussian process regression yields a learning-rate bound driven by information gain, clarifying when discovering “where the model works” is slow. Finally, we study how scaling interacts with discoverability: when calibrated signals and user mastery accelerate the harvesting of scale improvements, and when opacity can make gains from scaling effectively invisible.

中文摘要

生成式AI系统通常在看似“邻近”的任务上表现出高度不均衡的性能：它们可能在一个提示上表现出色，而在措辞或上下文仅有微小变化的另一个提示上却自信地犯错。我们将这种现象称为人工锯齿智能（AJI）。本文开发了一个易于处理的经济学模型来解释AJI，将采用视为一个信息问题：用户关心局部可靠性，但通常只能观察到粗略的全局质量信号。在一个基准的一维景观中，真实情况是一个粗糙的布朗过程，而模型“知道”从泊松过程中抽取的散点。模型进行最优插值，局部误差由后验方差衡量。我们推导出一个盲用用户的采用阈值，表明经验误差因检查悖论而被放大，并将规模法则解释为更密集的覆盖，这提高了平均质量但并未消除锯齿性。然后我们研究掌握和校准：一个能够根据局部不确定性进行条件判断的校准用户，即使在未通过盲用采用测试的领域也能获得正的期望值。将掌握建模为通过高斯过程回归学习可靠性图，会得到一个由信息增益驱动的学习率边界，阐明了何时发现“模型在何处有效”是缓慢的。最后，我们研究规模如何与可发现性相互作用：何时校准信号和用户掌握能加速规模改进的收获，以及何时不透明性会使规模带来的收益实际上不可见。
