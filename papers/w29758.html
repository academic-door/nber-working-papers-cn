Conditional Cash Transfers for Education

教育领域的有条件现金转移支付

Sandra García, Juan Saavedra

教育与劳动 全球/多国

Abstract

This chapter reviews the extensive literature to date on CCTs for education. Section 2 provides background on the origins and expansion of CCTs globally, and describes basic design features and variation in characteristics across programs. Section 3 presents a theory of change and an economic household decision-making model highlighting key comparative statics and empirical predictions for the introduction of an education CCT program. Section 4 discusses key methodological challenges in evaluating the impacts of education CCTs. Section 5 integrates and updates the extensive evidence to date on the impacts of education CCTs on various outcome domains over the life cycle, and provides the most comprehensive view to date on learning impacts by meta-analyzing new evidence from more than 30 studies—substantially more than prior reviews of the literature. Section 6 reviews the evidence on indirect and general equilibrium effects. Section 7 presents a simple model of costs commonly used in the literature, which we extend to analyze cost-effectiveness for a subset of programs. Section 8 concludes and highlights open questions for future research.

中文摘要

本章回顾了迄今为止关于教育领域有条件现金转移支付（CCT）的广泛文献。第2节提供了CCT在全球的起源和扩展背景，并描述了基本设计特征和不同项目之间的特征差异。第3节提出了一个变革理论和一个经济家庭决策模型，强调了引入教育CCT项目的关键比较静态和实证预测。第4节讨论了评估教育CCT影响的关键方法论挑战。第5节整合并更新了迄今为止关于教育CCT在生命周期内对各种结果领域影响的广泛证据，并通过元分析来自30多项研究的新证据——远多于先前的文献综述——提供了迄今为止关于学习影响的最全面视角。第6节回顾了关于间接和一般均衡效应的证据。第7节提出了一个文献中常用的简单成本模型，我们将其扩展以分析一部分项目的成本效益。第8节进行总结并强调了未来研究的开放性问题。
