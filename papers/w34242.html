Inward and Outward Migration under Shifting Legal-Democratic Regimes

法律-民主体制变迁下的向内与向外移民

Assaf Razin

国际贸易 全球/多国

Abstract

This paper analyzes the two-way relationship between international migration and political regime change, highlighting a feedback loop in which political shifts shape migration flows, while migration itself reshapes political trajectories. Relying on a Difference-in-Differences (DiD) framework and a dataset combining migration flows, regime quality indicators (CHRI), and measures of international integration, we identify three central results.

中文摘要

本文分析了国际移民与政治体制变迁之间的双向关系，突出了一个反馈循环：政治转变塑造移民流动，而移民本身又重塑政治轨迹。基于双重差分（DiD）框架以及一个结合了移民流动、体制质量指标（CHRI）和国际一体化衡量指标的数据集，我们确定了三个核心结果。
