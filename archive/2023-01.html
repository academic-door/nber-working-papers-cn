Inflationary Effects of Fiscal Support to Households and Firms

对家庭和企业财政支持的通货膨胀效应

Galina Hale, John C. Leer, Fernanda Nechio

Abstract

Fiscal support measures in response to the COVID-19 pandemic varied in their targeted beneficiaries. Relying on variability across 10 large economies, we study differences in the inflationary effects of fiscal support measures targeting consumers or businesses. Because conventional measures of real activity were distorted, we control for the underlying state of the real economy using households sentiment data. We find that fiscal support measures to consumers, but not firms, had inflationary effects that manifested 5 weeks following the announcement and peaked at 12 weeks. The magnitude of the effect was larger in an environment of improving consumer sentiment.

中文摘要

针对COVID-19疫情的财政支持措施在目标受益者上有所不同。利用10个大型经济体的差异性，我们研究了针对消费者或企业的财政支持措施在通货膨胀效应上的差异。由于传统的实际经济活动指标被扭曲，我们使用家庭情绪数据来控制实体经济的潜在状态。我们发现，针对消费者的财政支持措施（而非针对企业的措施）具有通货膨胀效应，这种效应在公告后5周显现，并在12周达到峰值。在消费者情绪改善的环境下，这种效应的规模更大。

The Marginal Disutility from Corruption in Social Programs: Evidence from Program Administrators and Beneficiaries

社会项目中腐败的边际负效用：来自项目管理员和受益者的证据

Arya Gaduh, Rema Hanna, Benjamin A. Olken

Abstract

Concerns about fraud in welfare programs common arguments worldwide against such programs. We conducted a survey experiment with over 28,000 welfare program administrators and over 19,000 beneficiaries in Indonesia to elicit the ‘marginal disutility from corruption,’ i.e., the trade-between more generous social assistance and losses due to corruption and fraud. Merely mentioning corruption reduced perceived program success, equivalent to distributing more than 20 percent less aid. However, respondents were not sensitive to the amount of corruption—respondents were willing to trade off $\$2$ of additional losses for an additional $\$1$ distributed to beneficiaries. Program administrators and beneficiaries had similar assessments.

中文摘要

对福利项目欺诈的担忧是全球范围内反对此类项目的常见论点。我们在印度尼西亚对超过28,000名福利项目管理员和超过19,000名受益者进行了一项调查实验，以引出“腐败的边际负效用”，即在更慷慨的社会援助与腐败和欺诈造成的损失之间的权衡。仅仅提及腐败就会降低感知的项目成功率，相当于减少了超过20%的援助分配。然而，受访者对腐败金额并不敏感——受访者愿意为每额外分配给受益者1美元而承受2美元的额外损失。项目管理员和受益者的评估相似。

Trading Places: Mobility Responses of Native and Foreign-Born Adults to the China Trade Shock

互换位置：本土和外国出生成年人对中国贸易冲击的流动性反应

David Autor, David Dorn, Gordon H. Hanson

Abstract

Previous research finds that the greater geographic mobility of foreign than native-born workers following economic shocks helps to facilitate local labor market adjustment to shifting regional economic conditions. We examine the role that immigration may have played in enabling U.S. commuting zones to respond to manufacturing job loss caused by import competition from China. Although population headcounts of the foreign-born fell by more than those of the native-born in regions exposed to the China trade shock, the overall contribution of immigration to labor market adjustment in this episode was small. Because most U.S. immigrants arrived in the country after manufacturing regions were already mature, few took up jobs in industries that would later see increased import penetration from China. The foreign-born share of the working-age population in regions with high trade exposure was only three-fifths that in regions with low exposure. Immigration thus appears more likely to aid adjustment to cyclical shocks, in which job loss occurs in regions that had recent booms in hiring, rather than facilitating adjustment to secular regional decline, in which hiring booms occurred in the more distant past.

中文摘要

先前的研究发现，在经济冲击后，外国出生工人的地理流动性高于本土出生工人，这有助于促进地方劳动力市场对区域经济状况变化的调整。我们考察了移民在帮助美国通勤区应对由中国进口竞争导致的制造业岗位流失方面可能发挥的作用。尽管在遭受中国贸易冲击的地区，外国出生人口数量下降幅度大于本土出生人口，但移民在这一事件中对劳动力市场调整的总体贡献很小。因为大多数美国移民是在制造业地区已经成熟之后才抵达美国的，很少有人从事后来中国进口渗透率增加的行业。在贸易暴露度高的地区，外国出生人口占劳动年龄人口的比例仅为低暴露度地区的五分之三。因此，移民似乎更可能有助于应对周期性冲击（即岗位流失发生在近期招聘繁荣的地区），而不是促进对长期区域衰退（即招聘繁荣发生在更久远的过去）的调整。

Peak-Hour Road Congestion Pricing: Experimental Evidence and Equilibrium Implications

高峰时段道路拥堵定价：实验证据与均衡含义

Gabriel Kreindler

Abstract

Developing country megacities suffer from severe road traffic congestion, yet the level of congestion is not a direct measure of equilibrium inefficiency. I study the peak-hour traffic congestion equilibrium in Bangalore. To measure travel preferences, I use a model of departure time choice to design a field experiment with congestion pricing policies and implement it using precise GPS data. Commuter responses in the experiment reveal moderate schedule inflexibility and a high value of time. I then show that in Bangalore, traffic density has a moderate and linear impact on travel delay. My policy simulations with endogenous congestion indicate that optimal congestion charges would lead to a small reduction in travel times, and small commuter welfare gains. This result is driven primarily by the shape of the congestion externality. Overall, these results suggest limited commuter welfare benefits from peak-spreading traffic policies in cities like Bangalore.

中文摘要

发展中国家的大城市饱受严重道路交通拥堵之苦，然而拥堵水平并非均衡无效率的直接衡量指标。我研究了班加罗尔的高峰时段交通拥堵均衡。为了衡量出行偏好，我使用了一个出发时间选择模型来设计一个包含拥堵定价政策的实地实验，并使用精确的GPS数据实施该实验。实验中通勤者的反应揭示了中度的日程不灵活性和较高的时间价值。然后我表明，在班加罗尔，交通密度对出行延误有适度且线性的影响。我的包含内生拥堵的政策模拟表明，最优拥堵收费将导致出行时间的小幅减少和通勤者福利的小幅增加。这一结果主要由拥堵外部性的形状驱动。总体而言，这些结果表明，在班加罗尔这样的城市，错峰交通政策带来的通勤者福利收益有限。

Exposure to Economic Distress during Pregnancy and Birth Outcomes

孕期经济困境暴露与出生结果

Mevlude Akbulut-Yuksel, Seyit M. Cilasun, Erdal Tekin, Belgi Turan

Abstract

This paper uses the severe economic crisis in Turkey in 2008-2009 as a quasi-experiment to evaluate the impact of worsening economic conditions during pregnancy on birthweight. Using birth history data from the 2008 and 2013 waves of the Demographic Health Surveys, we find that the economic crisis resulted in decreased birthweight in Turkey, especially impacting infants born to mothers with lower educational levels. Furthermore, a procyclical relationship exists between provincial income levels and the birthweight of infants born to mothers with lower levels of education. However, this relationship is only statistically significant during the crisis period. These results highlight how economic constraints on mothers with lower socio-economic status during economic crises can negatively affect birth outcomes. Furthermore, we examine shifts in fertility behavior and find a decrease in childbirth rates during the crisis, particularly in economically disadvantaged provinces. In line with this decrease in fertility, we also observe a reduced propensity to seek an abortion during the crisis period. Overall, these findings underscore the importance of understanding how economic crises affect infant health and the need for targeted interventions to support vulnerable populations, as well as addressing underlying socio-economic disparities to mitigate their impact on infant well-being.

中文摘要

本文利用土耳其2008-2009年严重的经济危机作为准实验，评估孕期经济状况恶化对出生体重的影响。使用2008年和2013年人口健康调查的生育史数据，我们发现经济危机导致土耳其出生体重下降，尤其对教育水平较低的母亲所生婴儿影响更大。此外，省级收入水平与低教育水平母亲所生婴儿的出生体重之间存在顺周期关系。然而，这种关系仅在危机期间具有统计显著性。这些结果凸显了经济危机期间对低社会经济地位母亲的经济约束如何对出生结果产生负面影响。此外，我们考察了生育行为的变化，发现危机期间生育率下降，尤其是在经济弱势省份。与生育率下降相一致，我们还观察到危机期间寻求堕胎的倾向降低。总体而言，这些发现强调了理解经济危机如何影响婴儿健康的重要性，以及需要有针对性的干预措施来支持弱势群体，并解决潜在的社会经济不平等以减轻其对婴儿福祉的影响。

An 'Austrian' Model of Global Value Chains

全球价值链的“奥地利”模型

Pol Antràs

Abstract

I develop a stylized model of multi-stage production in which the time length of each stage is endogenously determined. Letting the production process mature for a longer period of time increases labor productivity, but it comes at the cost of higher working capital needs for firms. Under autarky, countries with lower interest rates feature longer production processes, higher labor productivity, and higher wages. In a free trade equilibrium, countries with lower interest rates specialize in relatively ‘time intensive’ stages in global value chains (GVCs). Yet, if free trade brings about interest rate equalization, wages are also equalized and the pattern of trade is instead shaped by capital intensity and capital abundance, regardless of the time intensity of the various stages. Reductions in trade costs lead to patterns of specialization associated with higher amounts of vertical specialization in world trade. A worldwide decline in interest rates similarly fosters an increase in the share of GVC trade in world trade. The framework also sheds light on the role of trade credit and trade finance in shaping international specialization.

中文摘要

我开发了一个多阶段生产的简化模型，其中每个阶段的时间长度是内生的。让生产过程成熟更长时间会提高劳动生产率，但代价是企业需要更多的营运资金。在自给自足条件下，利率较低的国家拥有更长的生产过程、更高的劳动生产率和更高的工资。在自由贸易均衡中，利率较低的国家专门从事全球价值链中相对“时间密集型”的阶段。然而，如果自由贸易导致利率均等化，工资也会均等化，贸易模式则由资本密集度和资本丰裕度决定，而无论各阶段的时间密集度如何。贸易成本的降低会导致与全球贸易中更高垂直专业化程度相关的专业化模式。全球范围内的利率下降同样会促进全球价值链贸易占世界贸易份额的增加。该框架还揭示了贸易信贷和贸易融资在塑造国际专业化中的作用。

The Trickling Up of Excess Savings

超额储蓄的涓滴效应

Adrien Auclert, Matthew Rognlie, Ludwig Straub

Abstract

We provide a simple framework connecting the distribution of excess savings across households to the dynamics of aggregate demand. Deficit-financed fiscal transfers generate excess savings. The poorest households with the highest MPCs spend down their excess savings the fastest, increasing other households’ incomes and their excess savings. This leads to a long-lasting increase in aggregate demand until, ultimately, excess savings have “trickled up” to the richest savers with the lowest MPCs, raising wealth inequality.

中文摘要

我们提供了一个简单的框架，将家庭间超额储蓄的分布与总需求动态联系起来。赤字融资的财政转移支付产生了超额储蓄。拥有最高边际消费倾向的最贫困家庭最快地花掉他们的超额储蓄，从而增加了其他家庭的收入和他们的超额储蓄。这导致总需求持续增长，直到最终超额储蓄“涓滴”到拥有最低边际消费倾向的最富裕储蓄者手中，从而加剧了财富不平等。

The Market for CEOs: Evidence from Private Equity

CEO市场：来自私募股权的证据

Paul A. Gompers, Steven N. Kaplan, Vladimir Mukharlyamov

Abstract

Most research on the CEO labor market studies public company CEOs while largely ignoring CEOs in private equity (PE) funded companies. We fill this gap by studying the market for CEOs among U.S. companies purchased by PE firms in large leveraged buyout transactions. 71% of those companies hired new CEOs under PE ownership. More than 75% of the new CEOs are external hires with 67% being complete outsiders. These results are strikingly different from studies of public companies, particularly, Cziraki and Jenter (2022) who find that 72% of new CEOs in S&P 500 companies are internal promotions. The most recent experience of 67% of the outside CEOs was at a public company with almost 50% of external hires having some previous experience at an S&P 500 firm. We estimate the total compensation of buyout CEOs and find that it is much higher than that of CEOs of similarly sized public companies and slightly lower than that of S&P 500 CEOs. Overall, our results suggest that the broader market for CEOs is active and that, at least for PE funded portfolio companies, firm-specific human capital is relatively unimportant.

中文摘要

大多数关于CEO劳动力市场的研究都关注上市公司CEO，而很大程度上忽略了私募股权资助公司的CEO。我们通过研究私募股权公司在大型杠杆收购交易中收购的美国公司的CEO市场来填补这一空白。71%的此类公司在私募股权所有权下聘用了新CEO。超过75%的新CEO是外部招聘，其中67%是完全的外部人。这些结果与对上市公司的研究惊人地不同，特别是Cziraki和Jenter（2022）发现标普500公司中72%的新CEO是内部晋升。67%的外部CEO最近的工作经历是在一家上市公司，近50%的外部招聘人员曾在标普500公司工作过。我们估算了收购CEO的总薪酬，发现其远高于类似规模上市公司的CEO，略低于标普500公司的CEO。总体而言，我们的结果表明，更广泛的CEO市场是活跃的，并且至少对于私募股权资助的投资组合公司而言，公司特定人力资本相对不重要。

Corporate Valuation: An Empirical Comparison of Discounting Methods

公司估值：贴现方法的实证比较

Nicolas Hommel, Augustin Landier, David Thesmar

Abstract

The key purpose of corporate finance is to provide methods to compute the value of projects. The baseline textbook recommendation is to use the Present Value (PV) formula of expected cash flows, with a discount rate based on the CAPM. In this paper, we ask what is, empirically, the best discounting method. To do this, we study listed firms, whose actual prices and expected cash flows can be observed. We compare different discounting approaches on their ability to predict actual market prices. We find that discounting based on expected returns (such as variants on the CAPM or multi-factor model), performs very poorly. Discounting with an Implied Cost of Capital (ICC), imputed from comparable firms, obtains much better results. In terms of pricing methods, significant, but small, improvements can be obtained by allowing, in a simple and actionable way, for a more flexible term structure of expected returns. We benchmark all of our results with flexible, purely statistical models of prices based on Random Forest algorithms. These models do barely better than NPV-based methods. Finally, we show that under standard assumptions about the production function, the value loss from using the CAPM can be sizable, of the order of 10%.

中文摘要

公司金融的核心目的是提供计算项目价值的方法。教科书的基本建议是使用预期现金流的现值公式，贴现率基于资本资产定价模型。本文从实证角度探讨哪种贴现方法最佳。为此，我们研究上市公司，其实际价格和预期现金流可被观测。我们比较不同贴现方法预测实际市场价格的能力。我们发现，基于预期收益（如资本资产定价模型或多因子模型的变体）的贴现方法表现非常差。使用从可比公司推算出的隐含资本成本进行贴现，可获得更好的结果。在定价方法方面，通过一种简单且可操作的方式，允许预期收益的期限结构更具灵活性，可以带来显著但微小的改进。我们以基于随机森林算法的灵活纯统计价格模型作为所有结果的基准。这些模型的表现仅略优于基于净现值的方法。最后，我们证明，在关于生产函数的标准假设下，使用资本资产定价模型造成的价值损失可能相当大，约为10%。

A Theory of Fear of Floating

浮动恐惧理论

Javier Bianchi, Louphou Coulibaly

Abstract

Many central banks whose exchange rate regimes are classified as flexible are reluctant to let the exchange rate fluctuate. This phenomenon is known as “fear of floating”. We present a simple theory in which fear of floating emerges as an optimal policy outcome. The key feature of the model is an occasionally binding borrowing constraint linked to the exchange rate that introduces a feedback loop between aggregate demand and credit conditions. Contrary to the Mundellian paradigm, we show that a depreciation can be contractionary, and letting the exchange rate float can expose the economy to self-fulfilling crises.

中文摘要

许多汇率制度被归类为浮动的中央银行，却不愿让汇率波动。这一现象被称为“浮动恐惧”。我们提出一个简单理论，其中浮动恐惧作为最优政策结果出现。模型的关键特征是一个与汇率相关的、偶尔具有约束力的借贷约束，它在总需求和信贷条件之间引入了反馈循环。与蒙代尔范式相反，我们证明贬值可能具有紧缩效应，而让汇率浮动可能使经济暴露于自我实现的危机之中。

The Cost of Curbing Externalities with Market Power: Alcohol Regulations and Tax Alternatives

利用市场势力抑制外部性的成本：酒精监管与税收替代方案

Christopher Conlon, Nirupama L. Rao

Abstract

Products with negative externalities are often subject to regulations that limit competition. The single-product case may suggest that it is irrelevant for aggregate welfare whether output is re-stricted via corrective taxes or limiting competition. However, when products are differentiated, curbing consumption through market power can be costly. Firms with market power may not only reduce total quantity, but distort the purchase decisions of inframarginal consumers. We examine a common regulation known as post-and-hold (PH) used by a dozen states for the sale of alcoholic beverages. Theoretically, PH eliminates competitive incentives among wholesalers selling identical products. We assemble unique data on distilled spirits from Connecticut, including matched man-ufacturer and wholesaler prices, to evaluate the welfare consequences of PH. For similar levels of ethanol consumption, PH leads to substantially lower consumer welfare (and government revenue) compared to simple taxes, because it distorts consumption choices away from high-quality/premium brands and towards low-quality brands. Replacing PH with volumetric or ethanol-based taxes could reduce consumption by 10-11% without reducing consumer surplus, and roughly triple tax revenues.

中文摘要

具有负外部性的产品通常受到限制竞争的监管。单一产品案例可能表明，通过矫正性税收还是限制竞争来限制产出，对总福利而言无关紧要。然而，当产品存在差异化时，通过市场势力抑制消费可能代价高昂。拥有市场势力的企业不仅可能减少总数量，还会扭曲边际内消费者的购买决策。我们研究了一种常见的监管措施，即“报价并持有”，美国有十几个州在酒精饮料销售中采用该措施。从理论上讲，“报价并持有”消除了销售相同产品的批发商之间的竞争激励。我们收集了康涅狄格州蒸馏酒的独特数据，包括匹配的制造商和批发商价格，以评估“报价并持有”的福利后果。在乙醇消费水平相似的情况下，与简单的税收相比，“报价并持有”导致消费者福利（和政府收入）大幅降低，因为它将消费选择从高质量/高端品牌扭曲到低质量品牌。用基于体积或乙醇的税收取代“报价并持有”，可以在不减少消费者剩余的情况下将消费量减少10-11%，并使税收收入大约增加两倍。

Beyond Burnout: from Measuring to Forecasting

超越倦怠：从衡量到预测

Bart Blackburn, Tiffany Chan, Elizabeth Cherot, Richard B. Freeman, Xi Hu, Eric Matt, C. Aubrey Rhodes

Abstract

Burnout of physicians and other medical personnel is a major problem in the economics of healthcare systems, potentially costing billions of dollars. Knowledge of the determinants and costs of burnout at the organization level is sparse, making it difficult to assess the net benefits of interventions to reduce burnout at the level where arguably the greatest change can be affected. In this paper, we use data from a midsize healthcare organization with about 500 clinicians in 2021-22 to advance analysis of clinical burnout in two ways. First, we estimate the costs of clinician burnout beyond the widely studied losses due to turnover. Including hard-to-measure and potentially long-term costs that arise from reduced patient satisfaction and lower productivity of burnt-out clinicians at work, our analysis suggests a much higher cost of burnout per clinician than previous estimates that exclude these costs. Second, we use standard medical billing and administrative operating data to forecast turnover and productivity of clinicians to serve as an early warning system. Accurate estimates of both the cost of burnout now and of likely future costs should help decision-makers be proactive in their approach to solving the burnout crisis currently affecting the healthcare industry.
