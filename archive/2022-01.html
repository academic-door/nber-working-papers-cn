No. 1论文详情NBER w29691

Two-Way Fixed Effects and Differences-in-Differences with Heterogeneous Treatment Effects: A Survey

双向固定效应与异质性处理效应下的双重差分法：一项综述

Clément de Chaisemartin, Xavier D'Haultfoeuille

Abstract

Linear regressions with period and group fixed effects are widely used to estimate policies' effects: 26 of the 100 most cited papers published by the American Economic Review from 2015 to 2019 estimate such regressions. It has recently been show that those regressions may produce misleading estimates, if the policy's effect is heterogeneous between groups or over time, as is often the case. This survey reviews a fast-growing literature that documents this issue, and that proposes alternative estimators robust to heterogeneous effects.

中文摘要

带有时期和组别固定效应的线性回归被广泛用于估计政策效果：在2015年至2019年《美国经济评论》发表的100篇被引次数最高的论文中，有26篇使用了此类回归。近期研究表明，如果政策效应在组间或时间上存在异质性（这通常是常见情况），这些回归可能会产生误导性估计。本综述回顾了快速增长的文献，这些文献记录了这一问题，并提出了对异质性效应具有稳健性的替代估计方法。

No. 2论文详情NBER w29661

Health Insurance for Whom? The ‘Spill-up’ Effects of Children’s Health Insurance on Mothers

为谁提供健康保险？儿童健康保险对母亲的“向上溢出”效应

Daniel S. Grossman, Sebastian Tello-Trillo, Barton Willage

Abstract

A rich literature documents the benefits of social safety net programs for children. This paper focuses on an unexplored margin: how children’s programs impact parents’ well-being. We explore changes in children’s public health insurance and its effects on parents’ economic and behavioral outcomes. Using a simulated eligibility for Medicaid eligibility expansions in the 1980s and 1990s, we isolate variation in children’s Medicaid eligibility due to changes in government policies. We find that increases in children’s Medicaid eligibility increases the likelihood a mother is married, decreases her labor market participation, and reduces her smoking and alcohol consumption. Our findings suggest improved maternal well-being as measured by the Center for Epidemiological Studies-Depression score, a proxy for mental health. These results uncover a new link that provides an important mechanism, parental well-being, for interpreting the literature’s findings on the long-term, short-term, and intergenerational effects of Medicaid coverage.

中文摘要

大量文献记录了社会安全网项目对儿童的益处。本文关注一个尚未被探索的方面：儿童项目如何影响父母的福祉。我们探讨了儿童公共健康保险的变化及其对父母经济和行为结果的影响。利用20世纪80年代和90年代医疗补助资格扩展的模拟资格，我们分离出因政府政策变化导致的儿童医疗补助资格变动。我们发现，儿童医疗补助资格的增加提高了母亲已婚的可能性，降低了她的劳动市场参与率，并减少了她的吸烟和饮酒行为。我们的研究结果表明，以流行病学研究中心抑郁量表得分（心理健康代理指标）衡量的母亲福祉有所改善。这些结果揭示了一个新的联系，为解释文献中关于医疗补助覆盖的长期、短期和代际效应的发现提供了重要机制——父母福祉。

No. 3论文详情NBER w29625

Using Bid Rotation and Incumbency to Detect Collusion: A Regression Discontinuity Approach

利用投标轮换和现任优势检测合谋：一种断点回归方法

Kei Kawai, Jun Nakabayashi, Juan M. Ortner, Sylvain Chassang

Abstract

Cartels participating in procurement auctions frequently use bid rotation or prioritize incumbents to allocate contracts. However, establishing a link between observed allocation patterns and firm conduct has been difficult: there are cost-based competitive explanations for such patterns. We show that by focusing on auctions in which the winning and losing bids are very close, it is possible to distinguish allocation patterns reflecting cost differences across firms from patterns reflecting non-competitive environments. We apply our tests to two datasets: the sample of Ohio milk auctions studied in Porter and Zona (1999), and a sample of municipal procurement auctions from Japan.

中文摘要

参与采购拍卖的卡特尔经常使用投标轮换或优先考虑现任者来分配合同。然而，在观察到的分配模式与企业行为之间建立联系一直很困难：因为存在基于成本的竞争性解释可以说明此类模式。我们表明，通过关注胜出和失败投标非常接近的拍卖，可以区分反映企业间成本差异的分配模式与反映非竞争环境的分配模式。我们将我们的检验应用于两个数据集：Porter和Zona（1999）研究的俄亥俄州牛奶拍卖样本，以及来自日本的市政采购拍卖样本。
