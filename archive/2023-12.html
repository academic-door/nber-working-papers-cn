Innovation Booms, Easy Financing, and Human Capital Accumulation

创新热潮、宽松融资与人力资本积累

Johan Hombert, Adrien Matray

Abstract

Innovation booms are often fueled by easy financing that allows new technology firms to pay high wages that attracts skilled labor. Using the late 1990s Information and Communication Technology (ICT) boom as a laboratory, we show that skilled labor joining this new sector experienced sizeable long-term earnings losses. We show these earnings patterns are explained by faster skill obsolescence rather than either worker selection or the overall bust in the ICT sector. During the boom, financing flowed more to firms whose workers would experience the largest productivity declines, amplifying the negative effect of labor reallocation on aggregate human capital accumulation.

中文摘要

创新热潮通常由宽松融资推动，这使得新技术企业能够支付高薪以吸引技能劳动力。以20世纪90年代末的信息与通信技术（ICT）热潮为实验场景，我们发现加入这一新行业的技能劳动力经历了显著的长期收入损失。我们表明，这些收入模式是由技能更快过时所致，而非工人选择或ICT行业的整体衰退。在热潮期间，融资更多地流向了那些工人将经历最大生产率下降的企业，从而放大了劳动力重新配置对总体人力资本积累的负面效应。

Firms and Worker Health

企业与工人健康

Alexander Ahammer, Analisa Packham, Jonathan Smith

Abstract

We estimate the role of firms in worker health care utilization. Using linked administrative data on Austrian workers from 1998-2018, we exploit mobility between firms to estimate how much a firm contributes to worker-level differences in utilization in a setting with non-employer provided universal health care. We find that firms are responsible for nearly 30 percent of the variation in across-worker health care expenditures. Effects are not driven by changes in geography or industry. We then estimate a measure of relative firm-specific utilization and explore existing correlates to help explain these effects.

中文摘要

我们估计了企业在工人医疗保健利用中的作用。利用1998-2018年奥地利工人的关联行政数据，我们通过工人跨企业流动来估算，在非雇主提供的全民医保环境中，企业对工人层面医疗保健利用差异的贡献程度。我们发现，企业解释了工人间医疗保健支出差异的近30%。这些效应并非由地理或行业变化驱动。随后，我们估算了一个衡量企业特定相对利用率的指标，并探索了现有的相关因素以帮助解释这些效应。

Market Design for Surface Water

地表水市场设计

Billy A. Ferguson, Paul Milgrom

Abstract

Many proposed surface water transfers undergo a series of regulatory reviews designed to mitigate hydrological and economic externalities. While these reviews help limit externalities, they impose substantial transaction costs that also limit trade. To promote a well-functioning market for surface water in California, we describe how a new kind of water right and related regulatory practices can balance the trade-off between externalities and transaction costs, and how a Water Incentive Auction can incentivize a sufficient number of current rights holders to swap their old rights for the new ones. The Water Incentive Auction adapts lessons learned from the US government’s successful Broadcast Incentive Auction.

中文摘要

Financial Integration and Monetary Policy Coordination

金融一体化与货币政策协调

Javier Bianchi, Louphou Coulibaly

Abstract

Financial integration generates macroeconomic spillovers that may require international monetary policy coordination. We show that individual central banks may set nominal interest rates too low or too high relative to the cooperative outcome. We identify three sufficient statistics that determine whether the Nash equilibrium exhibits under-tightening or over-tightening: the output gap, sectoral differences in labor intensity, and the trade balance response to changes in nominal rates. We find that, independently of the shocks hitting the economy, under-tightening is possible during economic expansions or contractions. For large shocks, the gains from coordination can be substantial.

中文摘要

金融一体化会产生宏观经济溢出效应，可能需要国际货币政策协调。我们表明，相对于合作结果，各中央银行可能将名义利率设定得过低或过高。我们识别出三个决定纳什均衡是呈现紧缩不足还是紧缩过度的充分统计量：产出缺口、劳动密集度的部门差异以及贸易余额对名义利率变化的反应。我们发现，无论经济受到何种冲击，在经济扩张或收缩期间都可能出现紧缩不足。对于大规模冲击，协调的收益可能相当可观。

What Drives the Exchange Rate?

什么驱动汇率？

Oleg Itskhoki, Dmitry Mukhin

Abstract

We use a general open-economy wedge-accounting framework to characterize the set of shocks that can account for major exchange rate puzzles. Focusing on a near-autarky behavior of the economy, we show analytically that all standard macroeconomic shocks — including productivity, monetary, government spending, and markup shocks — are inconsistent with the broad properties of the macro exchange rate disconnect. News shocks about future macroeconomic fundamentals can generate plausible exchange rate properties. However, they show up prominently in contemporaneous asset prices, which violates the finance exchange rate disconnect. International shocks to trade costs, terms of trade and import demand, while potentially consistent with disconnect, do not robustly generate the empirical Backus-Smith, UIP and terms-of-trade properties. In contrast, the observed exchange rate behavior is consistent with risk-sharing (financial) shocks that arise from shifts in demand of foreign investors for home-currency assets, or vice versa.

中文摘要

我们使用一个一般性的开放经济楔子核算框架来刻画能够解释主要汇率谜题的一组冲击。聚焦于经济的近乎自给自足行为，我们分析表明，所有标准宏观经济冲击——包括生产率、货币、政府支出和加成率冲击——都与宏观汇率脱节的主要特征不一致。关于未来宏观经济基本面的新闻冲击可以产生合理的汇率特征。然而，它们会显著体现在同期资产价格中，这违反了金融汇率脱节。贸易成本、贸易条件和进口需求的国际冲击，虽然可能与脱节一致，但并不能稳健地产生经验性的Backus-Smith、UIP和贸易条件特征。相比之下，观察到的汇率行为与风险分担（金融）冲击一致，这些冲击源于外国投资者对本国货币资产需求的转变，反之亦然。

The Informational Centrality of Banks

银行的信息中心性

Nathan Foley-Fisher, Gary B. Gorton, Stéphane Verani

Abstract

The equity and debt prices of large nonbank firms contain information about the future state of the banking system. In this sense, banks are informationally central. The amount of this information varies over time and over equity and debt. During a financial crisis banks are, by definition of a crisis, at risk of failure. Debt prices became about 50 percent more informative than equity prices about the future state of the banking system during the financial crisis of 2007-2009. This was partly due to investors’ fears that banks might not be able to refinance their debt.

中文摘要

大型非银行企业的股票和债务价格包含关于银行体系未来状态的信息。从这个意义上说，银行具有信息中心性。这些信息的数量随时间以及股票和债务的不同而变化。在金融危机期间，根据危机的定义，银行面临倒闭风险。在2007-2009年金融危机期间，债务价格关于银行体系未来状态的信息含量比股票价格高出约50%。这部分是由于投资者担心银行可能无法为其债务进行再融资。

Wellbeing, Expectations and Unemployment in Europe

欧洲的福祉、预期与失业

David G. Blanchflower, Alex Bryson

Abstract

We find expectations are more sensitive to economic growth than traditional wellbeing metrics. We examine Eurobarometer micro data from 1973-2023 on movements in life satisfaction along with data from 1995-2022 on five expectations variables on and individual’s life and their financial and job situations plus their views on the economic and employment situation of their country in the year ahead. These expectations start to decline several months before the onset of downturns with especially large drops for the Great Recession and Covid. Annual GDP growth is positively associated with these expectations variables while it is uncorrelated with life satisfaction. The unemployment rate and the CPI reduce both. We analyze data for 29 European countries to predict changes in the unemployment rate 12 months ahead using individuals’ fears of unemployment in the presence of country and year fixed effects and lagged unemployment. We also use firms' expectations of future employment, which are also predictive of what happens to unemployment three months later. Using our preferred model specification, we present out-of-sample predictions that track actual movements in unemployment rates closely over a period in which there were two major recessions and unemployment shifted by a factor of two.

中文摘要

我们发现预期对经济增长的敏感度高于传统的福祉指标。我们考察了1973-2023年欧洲晴雨表微观数据中生活满意度的变化，以及1995-2022年关于个人生活、财务状况和就业状况的五项预期变量数据，加上他们对本国未来一年经济和就业状况的看法。这些预期在经济衰退开始前几个月就开始下降，其中大衰退和新冠疫情时期的降幅尤其大。年度GDP增长与这些预期变量呈正相关，而与生活满意度不相关。失业率和CPI则同时降低两者。我们分析了29个欧洲国家的数据，利用个人对失业的担忧，在包含国家和年份固定效应以及滞后失业率的情况下，预测未来12个月的失业率变化。我们还使用了企业对未来就业的预期，这些预期也能预测三个月后的失业情况。使用我们偏好的模型设定，我们给出了样本外预测，这些预测在两个重大衰退时期以及失业率变化幅度达两倍的时期内，紧密追踪了失业率的实际变动。

Migration Policy and the Supply of Foreign Physicians: Evidence from the Conrad 30 Waiver Program

移民政策与外国医生的供给：来自康拉德30豁免计划的证据

Breno Braga, Gaurav Khanna, Sarah Turner

Abstract

In the United States, rural and low-income communities have difficulty attracting and retaining physicians, potentially adversely impacting health outcomes. With a limited supply of physicians completing medical school at US universities, foreign-born and educated physicians provide a potential source of supply in underserved areas. For international medical school graduates (IMGs) the terms of the commonly used J-1 visa require a return to the home country for two years following employment in medical residency. Our analysis examines the extent to which the Conrad 30 Visa Waiver impacts the supply of physicians at state and local levels, particularly in areas designated as medically underserved. Changes in the federal limit on the number of waivers per state, combined with variation in the state-level restrictions on eligible specialties, and geographies in which physicians can work, provide evidence on the role of visa restrictions in limiting the supply of doctors. Expansion of the cap on visa waivers increased the supply of IMGs, particularly in states that did not limit waiver recipients to primary care physicians or particular places of employment. There is little evidence of reductions in US-trained doctors in states where IMG increases were the largest, suggesting little evidence for crowding out.

中文摘要

在美国，农村和低收入社区难以吸引和留住医生，这可能对健康结果产生不利影响。由于在美国大学完成医学院教育的医生供给有限，外国出生和受教育的医生为服务不足地区提供了潜在的供给来源。对于国际医学毕业生（IMG）而言，常用的J-1签证条款要求其在完成住院医师工作后返回母国两年。我们的分析考察了康拉德30签证豁免在多大程度上影响了州和地方层面的医生供给，特别是在被指定为医疗服务不足的地区。联邦对每州豁免数量的限制变化，加上各州对合格专业和医生可工作地理区域的限制差异，为签证限制在限制医生供给方面的作用提供了证据。签证豁免上限的扩大增加了IMG的供给，尤其是在那些不将豁免接受者限制在初级保健医生或特定工作地点的州。几乎没有证据表明，在IMG增幅最大的州，美国本土培训的医生有所减少，这表明几乎没有挤出效应。

Universal Portfolio Shrinkage

通用投资组合收缩

Bryan T. Kelly, Semyon Malamud, Mohammad Pourmohammadi, Fabio Trojani

Abstract

We introduce a nonlinear covariance shrinkage method for building optimal portfolios. Our universal portfolio shrinkage approximator (UPSA) is given in closed-form, is cheap to implement, and improves upon existing shrinkage methods. Rather than uniformly penalizing all principal components of returns or discarding low-variance ones, UPSA instead reweights components to explicitly optimize expected out-of-sample portfolio performance. In empirical applications using a large cross-section of anomaly factors, it delivers robust improvements over alternative shrinkage methods in the literature.

中文摘要

我们引入了一种用于构建最优投资组合的非线性协方差收缩方法。我们的通用投资组合收缩近似器（UPSA）以闭式形式给出，实现成本低廉，并且优于现有的收缩方法。UPSA并非对所有主成分进行统一惩罚或丢弃低方差成分，而是重新加权各成分，以明确优化样本外投资组合的预期表现。在使用大量横截面异象因子的实证应用中，与文献中的替代收缩方法相比，它提供了稳健的改进。

Valuing Technology Complementarities: Rooftop Solar and Energy Storage

评估技术互补性：屋顶太阳能与储能

Bryan Bollinger, Naim Darghouth, Kenneth Gillingham, Andres Gonzalez-Lira

Abstract

Product complementarities can shape market patterns, influencing the demand for related products and their accessories. This study examines complementarities in the demand for rooftop solar and an accessory, battery energy storage. Using nationwide administrative data, we estimate a dynamic nested-logit model of solar and storage adoption. We quantify the demand complementarity between solar and storage, and find that if storage was not available, 20% of households who coadopt solar and storage would not adopt anything. We find that the demand for solar and storage bundles increases with power outages, with a larger effect in California.

Organized Crime and Economic Growth: Evidence from Municipalities Infiltrated by the Mafia

有组织犯罪与经济增长：来自被黑手党渗透市镇的证据

Alessandra Fenizia, Raffaele Saggio

Abstract

This paper studies the long-run economic impact of dismissing city councils infiltrated by organized crime. Applying a matched difference-in-differences design to the universe of Italian social security records, we find that city council dismissals (CCDs) increase employment, the number of firms, and industrial real estate prices. The effects are concentrated in Mafia-dominated sectors and in municipalities where fewer incumbents are re-elected. The dismissals generate large economic returns by weakening the Mafia and fostering trust in local institutions. The analysis suggests that CCDs represent an effective intervention for establishing legitimacy and spurring economic activity in areas dominated by organized crime.

Asset Demand of U.S. Households

美国家庭的资产需求

Xavier Gabaix, Ralph S. J. Koijen, Federico Mainardi, Sangmin Oh, Motohiro Yogo

Abstract

We use novel monthly security-level data on U.S. household portfolio holdings, flows, and returns to analyze asset demand across an extensive range of asset classes, including both public and private assets. Our dataset covers a broad range of households across the wealth distribution, notably including 439 billionaires. This ensures representation of ultra-high-net-worth (UHNW) households that are typically not well covered in survey data. With these data, we study the portfolio rebalancing behavior of households and ask whether (and, if so, which) households play an important stabilizing role in financial markets. Our findings reveal a stark contrast: less affluent households sell U.S. equities amid market downturns, while UHNW households buy and contribute to stabilizing markets. This behavior is more pronounced among households who rebalance their portfolios more frequently. However, the sensitivity of flows to returns is generally quite small and as the trades of different wealth groups partly offset each other, the aggregate household sector plays a limited role to absorb financial fluctuations. To understand the contrasting trading behavior across households, we show that a household’s flows to U.S. equities are negatively correlated with its “active returns” (the difference between an investor’s return and the market return). However, the flows to U.S. equities of less affluent households are also positively correlated with broad market returns – perhaps due to shifts in risk aversion, sentiment, or perceived macroeconomic risk – leading this group of households to act pro-cyclically. Across all asset classes, three factors with intuitive economic interpretations explain 81% of all variation in portfolio rebalancing. Those factors bet on the long-term equity premium, the credit premium, and the premium on municipal bonds. In sum, our framework paints a quantitative picture of U.S. households’ assets and rebalancing marked by a great deal of insensitivity and inertia throughout the distribution, even for UHNW households. These new facts are useful for the calibration of macro-finance models with heterogeneous households and multiple risky asset classes.

中文摘要

我们利用美国月度证券层面的家庭投资组合持有、资金流和回报数据，分析了涵盖包括公共和私人资产在内的广泛资产类别的资产需求。我们的数据集覆盖了财富分布中广泛的家庭，特别包括439位亿万富翁。这确保了通常调查数据中覆盖不足的超高净值（UHNW）家庭的代表性。利用这些数据，我们研究了家庭的投资组合再平衡行为，并探究家庭是否（如果是，哪些家庭）在金融市场中扮演重要的稳定角色。我们的发现揭示了鲜明的对比：较不富裕的家庭在市场低迷时卖出美国股票，而超高净值家庭则买入并有助于稳定市场。这种行为在更频繁地再平衡其投资组合的家庭中更为明显。然而，资金流对回报的敏感度通常非常小，并且由于不同财富群体的交易部分相互抵消，整体家庭部门在吸收金融波动方面作用有限。为了理解不同家庭之间对比鲜明的交易行为，我们表明，一个家庭对美国股票的资金流与其“主动回报”（投资者回报与市场回报之差）呈负相关。然而，较不富裕家庭对美国股票的资金流也与广泛的市场回报正相关——可能是由于风险厌恶、情绪或感知到的宏观经济风险的变化——导致这一群体家庭表现出顺周期行为。在所有资产类别中，三个具有直观经济解释的因素解释了投资组合再平衡中81%的变异。这些因素押注于长期股权溢价、信用溢价和市政债券溢价。总之，我们的框架描绘了美国家庭资产和再平衡的量化图景，其特点是整个分布中（甚至对于超高净值家庭）存在大量的不敏感性和惯性。这些新事实对于校准具有异质性家庭和多种风险资产类别的宏观金融模型非常有用。

An IV Hazard Model of Loan Default with an Application to Subprime Mortgage Cohorts

贷款违约的IV风险模型：次级抵押贷款队列的应用

Christopher Palmer

Abstract

This paper develops a control-function methodology accounting for endogenous or mismeasured regressors in hazard models. I provide sufficient identifying assumptions and regularity conditions for the estimator to be consistent and asymptotically normal. Applying the estimator to the subprime mortgage crisis, I quantify what caused the foreclosure rate to triple across the 2003-2007 subprime cohorts. To identify the elasticity of default with respect to housing prices, I use various home-price instruments including historical variation in home-price cyclicality. Loose credit played a significant role in the crisis, but much of the increase in defaults across cohorts was caused by home-price declines unrelated to lending standards, with a 10% decline in home prices increasing subprime mortgage default rates by 50%.

Population and Welfare: Measuring Growth when Life is Worth Living

人口与福利：当生命值得活时衡量增长

Mohamad Adhami, Mark Bils, Charles I. Jones, Peter J. Klenow

Abstract

Economic growth is typically measured in per capita terms. A long tradition in philosophy, however, suggests that social welfare may depend on the number of people as well. To illustrate how much this matters quantitatively, we decompose social welfare growth—measured in consumption-equivalent (CE) units — into contributions from rising population and rising per capita consumption.
